### 2) Turkish Peshtemal / Fouta Bath Towel (single or 2-pack)

- **Why worth researching:** A peshtemal is a lightweight, flat-woven cotton towel used as bath towel, hammam towel, beach towel, or throw. It solves a real Amazon.es customer complaint about terry towels being bulky, slow-drying, and heavy in Spanish apartments with limited drying space. It also compresses well → low FBA fees vs. terry.
- **Demand signals:** Steady year-round with a summer beach uplift; recognisable niche among the "sustainable / apartment living / travel" buyer.

- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Origin ("100% Turkish cotton") claimed without a certificate or invoice.
  - Colour photos differ from delivered colour (common review complaint).
  - Sizes shown in imperial or unclear (needs cm, in Spanish).

  - Tassels report as fraying after 2–3 washes.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish honest GSM (target 320–380 g/m²) and dimension in cm.
  - Reinforced tassel binding (small, documented change).
  - 3–4 muted colours only (don't chase the 12-colour trend).
  - Recyclable kraft belly-band with washing instructions in Spanish/Portuguese/English.
  - "One towel, four uses" listing angle (bath / beach / sofá / picnic).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as single towel €19.90; add a 2-pack (mixed neutrals) €34.90 once reviews are stable.

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Weighted Blanket (*manta con peso / manta pesada*)
- **Why rejected:** Heavy (5–10 kg) → very high FBA size tier and shipping cost. High return risk (buyers dislike weight / heat once received). Dominated by a few established brands with 10k+ reviews. Marginal home-textile fit but poor economics for a first Rosanube launch. Also exceeds config's "avoid very heavy / bulky" rule.

### B) Silk Pillowcase (*funda almohada seda*)
- **Why rejected:** Category increasingly dominated by established beauty-adjacent brands. Consumers expect certificates (mommie / momme, OEKO-TEX) and origin proof — per config compliance rules, Rosanube cannot make those claims without documented certificates. Very high return risk from colour/feel/authenticity complaints. High cost per unit → price pressure.

### C) Memory-Foam Bath Mat (*alfombra baño memory foam*)
- **Why rejected:** Amazon.es sub-category already anchored by Amazon Basics and a few generic Chinese brands racing prices to €8–€12. High complaint rates on odour, slipping, and mould. Bulkier polybag → higher FBA fee than a flat waffle towel or standard bath mat. Weak differentiation opportunity for a private label without going into questionable "antibacterial" claims.

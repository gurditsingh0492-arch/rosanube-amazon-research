### 2) Cotton Percale Duvet Cover Set — solid neutrals (Funda Nórdica Percal)

- **Spanish keyword:** *funda nórdica percal algodón*, *funda nórdica 150 algodón*, *juego funda nórdica*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Dormitorio › Fundas nórdicas
- **Why worth researching:** Duvet covers ("fundas nórdicas") are the flagship bedroom textile in Spain — every Spanish adult owns at least one, and the segment is dominated on Amazon.es by cheap microfibre / poliéster listings and a few premium brands (Zara Home, El Corte Inglés). There is a clean gap between €20 poly and €70+ premium cotton for **honest 100% cotton percale at €35–€49** in solid Iberian neutrals. Bed-refresh peaks September (vuelta al cole) and January (rebajas / winter refresh) — starting supplier research now sets up for Q3–Q4.
- **Demand signals:** Very high, steady year-round with two visible peaks (Sept, Jan). Bed refresh is repeat-purchase over a 3–5 year cycle plus adult independence / new home events.
- **Competition signals:** Present but **stratified**, not dominated. Amazon Basics anchors the microfibre bottom; premium slots are held by branded home. The €30–€50 honest-cotton private-label slot is under-served and often filled by unclear-composition imports.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fibre composition vague ("algodón" in title, "50% poliéster" in fine print).
  - Thread count / weave type undeclared — customers cannot distinguish percale from satin.
  - Sizes and pillowcase inclusion inconsistent (some sets ship *funda* only, no *funda de almohada*).
  - Colour photos over-saturated → real product looks duller in reviews.
  - Complaints: shrinkage after first wash, wrinkling, thin feel.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real fibre composition (100% cotton percale), thread count (target ~200 TC), and pre-washed status.
  - **Explicit set contents in the title and image #1**: 1× duvet cover + 2× pillowcases 45×85 (Spanish standard). No ambiguity.
  - 4–5 muted neutrals only — natural, arena, salvia, terracotta, blanco roto. No trend colours.
  - Photograph in a real Iberian bedroom (terracotta tile, wood, natural light) — not white studio.
  - Spanish-native listing copy, care label + fibre label + Spanish size label.
  - Kraft-and-cotton-band packaging → giftable for Reyes / Navidad without extra cost.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **one size first** — 150 cm (Spain's most common single/queen for wide beds). Add 135 cm (single) and 240 cm (king) later. Do **not** launch all three sizes and all colours simultaneously — that multiplies FBA storage and photography cost.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** — size variation is the main risk (need to control SKU sprawl); returns can rise if colour photos are inaccurate.
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 9/10
  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 8/10
  - Margin potential: 7/10 (higher AOV, higher COGS)
  - Risk: 5/10 (size complexity + colour-accuracy return risk)

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Electric Heated Blanket (*manta eléctrica*)
- **Why rejected:** Explicit config violation — **electronics + battery-adjacent + safety certification**. Requires CE / LVD / EMC compliance, GPSR responsible person, mandatory Spanish safety documentation, return risk on electrical failure, brand-dominated by established Spanish/EU manufacturers (Daga, Imetec, Beurer). Zero brand fit for Rosanube (a textile-honesty brand), high liability exposure. **NO GO / Rejected**.

### B) Baby Muslin Swaddle / Cloths (*muselinas bebé*, *arrullo bebé*)
- **Why rejected:** Baby-textile category triggers heavy compliance: EN 71 chemical / mechanical safety, REACH, OEKO-TEX practically required, dedicated GPSR file, high buyer scrutiny, and permanent liability exposure. Return risk is elevated (buyers reject any perceived odour, colour bleed, or loose thread). Rosanube brand story is adult home linen, not infant care. Even though muslin is textile-adjacent, this belongs to a specialist baby brand — not a first-year private label. **NO GO / Rejected**.

### C) Faux Fur / Sherpa Throw Blanket (*manta pelo sintético*, *manta borrego*)
- **Why rejected:** The €10–€25 slot is a **race to the bottom** dominated by very cheap Chinese imports and a few well-established home brands (Pieles del Sur, Textiles Mora). Common review complaints: heavy shedding, static, cheap look after 2 washes — high return risk. Fashion-trend adjacent, high FBA fee (bulky compressed polybag), weak Rosanube brand fit (synthetic, not the natural-fibre voice). Muslin throw (SKU #3 above) is a strictly better direction for the same shelf. **NO GO / Rejected**.

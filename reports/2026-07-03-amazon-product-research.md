### 1) Muslin / Gauze Cotton Bath Towel for Adults (4-layer)

- **Spanish keyword:** *toalla muselina algodón adulto*, *toalla gasa algodón*, *toalla baño muselina*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Baño › Toallas de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Muslin/gauze towels (4- or 6-layer cotton gauze) are the fast-drying, lightweight alternative to terry — perfect for small Spanish apartments, travel, and warm-climate use. The muslin/gauze towel is already normalised in the baby aisle on Amazon.es (Aden+Anais etc.) but the **adult** version is thinly represented. Sits directly next to Rosanube's waffle/peshtemal aesthetic and repurposes the same neutral palette.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Consistent presence in "quick-dry" and "toalla ligera" search results; category has steady growth in the "small apartment / minimalist / travel" buyer segment; not weather-seasonal.

- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - "Muselina" in title but photo is clearly terry — mislabelling complaints.
  - Layer count (3-layer vs 4-layer vs 6-layer) buried or missing.

  - Images shot only on white — no lifestyle/bath context.
  - Only baby sizes shown; adult buyers cannot tell if the towel is big enough.

  - Publish honest **4-layer gauze**, **~350 g/m²**, and dimensions **90×150 cm** (bath) and **50×90 cm** (hand) in cm.

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Electric Heated Blanket (*manta eléctrica calefactora*)
- **Why rejected:** Electronics + heating element = compliance-heavy (CE/LVD/EMC certification, safety file, GPSR responsible person exposure, EPR-EEE electronics), high return-risk category (temperature complaints, safety fears), and dominated by established European electrical brands. Violates config rules "Avoid electronics", "Products requiring complicated certifications", and "Products with unclear legal or safety restrictions". Not a Rosanube fit.

### B) Bamboo Bedsheet Set (*juego sábanas bambú*)
- **Why rejected:** Category is dominated by established bedding brands with 10k+ reviews, price race is aggressive, and the core "bambú" fibre claim has a **known EU labelling problem** — most so-called "bamboo" is regenerated cellulose (viscose) and must be labelled as such under EU textile regulation. Compliance risk is high without a documented mill certificate. Also many sizes × many colours = variation explosion, against the config rule "Products with too many sizes, colours, or variations".

### C) Weighted Lap Pad / Weighted Cushion (*cojín con peso, manta pesada regazo*)
- **Why rejected:** Small format of the weighted-blanket category. Health-claim adjacency (anxiety, calm, autism, sensory) creates a real compliance/medical-claim risk — the config explicitly forbids therapeutic / medical / orthopedic claims without documentation. Heavy relative to size → poor FBA fee tier. High return risk once the buyer feels the weight. Weak Rosanube brand fit.

## Executive Summary

Today's five ideas cluster around **table textiles** and **replenishment cleaning textiles** — categories where private-label sellers on Amazon.es routinely outsell big brands because purchase decisions are driven by price, pack size, honest specs, and Spanish-language listing quality rather than brand equity.

Common threads across the shortlist:

- Non-seasonal, replenishment-friendly demand (cleaning cloths, napkins, aprons, placemats, bath mats).
- Fragmented competition — mostly small private labels with weak listings; few dominant EU brands.
- Simple size/colour variation trees (one size, 3–4 muted colours).
- Standard EU textile compliance (fibre composition label + care label + Spanish-language + GPSR + EPR). No organic / OEKO-TEX / hypoallergenic / antibacterial claims unless certificates are on file.
- Preferred price band **€15–€35** where a well-packed multi-unit pack lands.

**None** of today's five is recommended for a purchase order. All are proposed as **Further Research** or **Watchlist** pending Helium 10, Keepa, and real supplier quotes.

## Top 5 Product Opportunities

### 1) Cotton Placemats — 4-pack, Linen-Look or Waffle Weave

- **Spanish keywords:** *individuales de mesa tela*, *salvamanteles tela algodón*, *manteles individuales lino lavado*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Textiles de mesa › Salvamanteles / Manteles individuales
- **Why worth researching:** Table placemats are a low-return-risk, repeat-purchase table textile. Amazon.es listings are dominated either by cheap plastic/PVC placemats (aesthetic complaints) or by generic linen-look private labels with poor Spanish copy and uncertain fabric composition. A clean, honestly-specified 4-pack in a "clean Iberian home" palette can slot in around €18–€26 without needing model photography.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to verify):** Steady daily demand tied to family dinners, gifting, and home-restaurant/hosting trend; no strong seasonality (light Christmas / Reyes uplift). Multiple listings in the sub-category with 3-figure to 4-figure review counts, consistent Best Sellers presence for tela / lino variants.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. Top 30 slots mostly private-label sellers, price band €12–€28 for 4-pack, review counts often 100–1,500. Few brand household names.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fabric composition often "algodón/poliéster" without percentage.
  - GSM / weight rarely stated.
  - Photos white-background, no dining lifestyle context.
  - Placemat size labelled inconsistently (30×45, 33×48, 35×50 all common — customers complain about mismatch).
  - Complaints about staining, wrinkling, and non-machine-washable disclosure buried in bullets.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real composition (100 % cotton or 55/45 linen-cotton), GSM, and stated size in cm.
  - Muted, brand-consistent palette (natural, salvia, terracotta, off-white) — 4 identical colour or 4-mixed neutral pack.
  - Dining lifestyle photos on Spanish/Iberian ceramic (not Nordic beige everywhere).
  - Reinforced double-stitched hem; pre-washed to reduce first-wash shrink complaints.
  - Kraft belly-band + Spanish care card (giftable without extra unit cost).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **one SKU** = 4-pack, mixed neutrals, ~33×48 cm. Later add a 6-pack "hosting" edition and a matching *camino de mesa* cross-sell (already on the July 1 pipeline).
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium**
- **Score /50:**

  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 8/10
  - Margin potential: 7/10 (light, compact, easy FBA)

### 2) Unisex Cotton Kitchen Apron (Delantal de Cocina)

- **Spanish keywords:** *delantal cocina*, *delantal cocina algodón*, *delantal chef mujer hombre*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Textiles de cocina › Delantales
- **Why worth researching:** Aprons sit right at the intersection of Rosanube's kitchen-textile lane and a gift-friendly SKU. They avoid the sizing complexity of apparel (one adult size + adjustable neck/waist ties covers most buyers), while enjoying steady replenishment and gift-season lifts (Día de la Madre, Día del Padre, Navidad). Amazon.es listings are heavy on cheap polyester "chef costume" aprons and light on honest cotton canvas / linen-look grown-up aesthetic.
- **Demand signals:** Steady year-round; noticeable spikes around gift dates but not a purely seasonal SKU. Recognisable keyword: *delantal cocina* is a top intent term with clear buying intent.
- **Competition signals:** Present but fragmented. Top slots mix cheap €8–€12 polyester and premium €25–€40 canvas / linen. The €18–€25 "grown-up, honest cotton" segment feels under-served. Review counts often 200–3,000 for entrenched listings.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fabric described vaguely ("algodón grueso") without composition percentage or GSM.
  - Ties too short / not adjustable enough — repeat review complaint.
  - Pocket stitching fails after a few washes.
  - Colours in photos differ from delivered product (dark navy showing as black, etc.).
  - Spanish copy often machine-translated.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - 100 % cotton canvas, published GSM (target ~240–280 g/m²).
  - Adjustable neck buckle (metal or coconut button) + reinforced waist ties long enough for larger sizes.
  - Two reinforced front pockets + towel loop.
  - 3–4 muted colours (natural, terracotta, salvia, charcoal) — no cartoon/print variants.
  - Optional embroidered "hola cocina" or minimal Rosanube tag for gift positioning (low-cost premium cue).
  - Spanish care label + fibre composition + country of origin.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as single apron €19.90–€22.90. Later add a **2-pack "his & hers" mixed neutrals** at €34.90 and a **matching kitchen-towel bundle** cross-sell (waffle towels from July 1 pipeline).
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium**
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 7/10
  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 8/10
  - Margin potential: 7/10
  - Risk: 8/10 (low return risk if one-size-adjustable and honest sizing chart)
  - **Total: 36/50**

### 3) Microfiber Cleaning Cloths — 10-pack Multi-Colour (Bayetas Microfibra)

- **Spanish keywords:** *bayetas microfibra*, *paños microfibra limpieza*, *trapos microfibra pack*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Productos de limpieza › Bayetas y paños / Ropa de hogar › Textiles de cocina › Bayetas
- **Why worth researching:** True replenishment SKU — customers rebuy every 3–9 months. Sales are highly fragmented, price-sensitive, and driven by **honest pack composition + colour coding for hygiene use cases** (kitchen / bathroom / glass / floor). Low FBA fee tier because light and compact. Ideal for a "quiet compounder" listing that runs on repeat customers and organic ranking rather than trend chasing.
- **Demand signals:** Consistently in the top slots of *limpieza / bayetas*; steady non-seasonal demand; low review-per-unit-sold ratio (customers buy repeatedly but don't always review — good signal for hidden volume once ranked).
- **Competition signals:** Very fragmented but **crowded**. Bounty-style multipacks from Amwan / AmazonBasics / Vileda anchor the top; below them there's a long tail of private labels at 6-pack / 10-pack / 20-pack. Price band €6–€18. Margin risk is real if landed cost is not carefully controlled.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - "Microfibra" quality inconsistent — customers complain about linting, poor absorption, poor durability after 20–30 washes.
  - GSM rarely stated (target 300 g/m² for kitchen / 250 g/m² for glass).
  - Colour coding not explained (customers don't know which colour to use where).
  - Pack composition unclear.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - **Colour-coded hygiene system**: 4 blue (kitchen), 3 green (bathroom), 2 yellow (glass), 1 grey (floor) — printed on packaging and product tag with Spanish + English explanation. This is a real product-management differentiator, not a marketing claim.
  - Published GSM per cloth type + real dimensions in cm.
  - Reinforced edge stitching for wash durability.
  - Recyclable kraft sleeve rather than plastic overwrap (EPR-friendly and photographs well).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Single SKU = 10-pack colour-coded. Later add a 20-pack "family" and a 5-pack "starter." Avoid launching multiple pack sizes on day one — cannibalizes and dilutes reviews.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** (margin pressure — supplier cost has to be tight and freight optimized)
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 8/10
  - Competition: 5/10 (crowded and price-competitive)
  - Differentiation: 7/10 (colour-coded system is real)
  - Margin potential: 6/10 (thin per unit, needs volume)
  - Risk: 7/10
  - **Total: 33/50**

- **Why worth researching:** Reusable cloth napkins are a **quiet growth niche** on Amazon.es driven by (a) sustainability/anti-single-use trends, (b) hosting / long-lunch culture, and (c) HORECA-lite buyers (small B&Bs, casas rurales). Standard size (40×40 cm), non-seasonal, low return risk, easy to stack for FBA. Complements Rosanube's placemats and table-runner pipeline.
- **Demand signals:** Steady daily demand; noticeable Best Sellers presence for tela / algodón packs of 6 and 12; keyword volume for *servilletas tela* is meaningful year-round with light Christmas uplift.

  - Optional kraft ribbon for gifting.

### 5) Cotton Bath Mat — Anti-Slip Backing, 50×80 cm (Alfombra de Baño Algodón)

- **Spanish keywords:** *alfombra baño algodón*, *alfombra baño antideslizante algodón*, *alfombrilla baño rizo*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Bath mats are a **replenishment SKU** with a well-known set of Amazon customer complaints: cheap microfiber/memory-foam options mildew, shed, and lose their non-slip backing within months. A cotton rizo bath mat with a documented latex anti-slip backing and honest GSM sits in a defensible middle band around €18–€28, avoiding both the memory-foam segment (July 1 rejected) and the premium designer segment.
- **Demand signals:** Consistent year-round; light spike around housing/reforma season (spring, September). Recognisable keyword *alfombra baño* dominates entry-level searches; long-tail terms *alfombra baño algodón* and *alfombra baño antideslizante* have more buying intent.
- **Competition signals:** Present but not consolidated. Top slots mix cheap €7–€12 microfiber, Amazon Basics-style cotton at €12–€18, and premium designer €30–€60. The €18–€28 "honest cotton rizo with real anti-slip" band feels under-served.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Anti-slip backing weak, peels off after a few washes.
  - GSM not stated (target 1500–1800 g/m² for cotton rizo bath mat).
  - Colours differ from photos.
  - Size labelled inconsistently — customers complain 50×80 shows up as 45×75.
  - Complaints of shedding, mildew, and stiffening after washes.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - 100 % cotton rizo, published GSM (target ~1600 g/m²).
  - Real 50×80 cm, verified in production.
  - Documented latex anti-slip backing with a **stated tested wash count** ("mantiene antideslizante hasta X lavados a 40 °C") — evidence-based, not "hypoallergenic"-style unverified claim.
  - Reinforced edge binding to reduce fraying.
  - 3–4 muted colours (natural, salvia, terracotta, off-white) — no bright multi-print.
  - Care label in Spanish + fibre composition + REACH-compliant backing statement.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch single SKU 50×80 cm. Later add a 2-piece bathroom set (50×80 mat + a smaller pedestal mat) once the main SKU is stable. Avoid launching 5 sizes at once.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** (backing durability is the one thing that can generate 1-star reviews at scale — sample validation is essential)
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 8/10
  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 7/10 (backing durability is a real, testable differentiator)
  - Margin potential: 7/10
  - Risk: 6/10 (backing failure is the main risk)
  - **Total: 34/50**

## Do Not Pursue Today

### A) Electric Blanket / Heated Throw — *manta eléctrica*
- **Reason for rejection:** Explicitly excluded by `research_config.md` — electronics, batteries/heating element, safety certification (CE + EMC + LVD), fire/burn liability, high return risk, dangerous-goods logistics. Sits outside Rosanube's textile brand focus. Big brands (Beurer, Medisana, IMETEC) dominate. **NO GO.**

### B) Silk / Satin Pillowcase — *funda almohada seda / satén*
- **Reason for rejection:** Already flagged on the July 1 Do Not Pursue list. Colour accuracy is a chronic 1-star complaint driver; hair/beauty positioning drifts into cosmetic-adjacent claims Rosanube can't substantiate; margin gets squeezed by real mulberry silk cost or by "satén" mis-branding risk. **Watchlist only — do not pursue.**

### C) Duvet Cover Set / Funda Nórdica — *funda nórdica 150 / 135 / 180*
- **Reason for rejection:** Classic **variation-explosion trap** — Spanish bed sizes 90 / 105 / 135 / 150 / 160 / 180 × multiple colours × pattern options = 20–40 SKUs before selling the first unit. Return risk is high (colour/size mismatch complaints are the #1 review theme). Compliance is more demanding (fibre composition, thread count claims). Dominant EU brands (Amazon Basics, Vivaraise, Utopia Bedding, Douceur d'intérieur) anchor the top. Rosanube's operational bandwidth is better spent on flat/2-panel textiles first. **NO GO for launch — Watchlist for post-scale revisit.**

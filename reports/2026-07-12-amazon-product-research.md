## Top 5 Product Opportunities

### 1) Muslin / Double-Gauze Kitchen Cloth Set (4-layer, 6-pack)

- **Spanish keyword:** *paños de cocina muselina*, *trapos algodón muselina 4 capas*, *paños cocina gasa*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Textiles de cocina › Paños de cocina
- **Why worth researching:** Muslin / 4-layer double-gauze cloths (Japanese / Korean-style *sarashi*) are a differentiated alternative to standard terry or waffle. They dry glassware without lint, fold flat (low FBA cube), and photograph as a premium "artisanal" product without a premium unit cost. Amazon.es catalogue is currently thin on Spanish-language, well-photographed 6-packs.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — verify):** Steady replenishment demand from the "quality kitchen" segment (baking, coffee, wine service). Not seasonal. Related keyword "paños vajilla sin pelusa" pulls consistent traffic on Amazon.es.
- **Competition signals:** Present but shallow — mostly generic sellers listing 2-packs at €8–€12 with poor Spanish copy; a handful of French / Italian small brands. Review counts often 50–800 → realistic entry.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Layer count (2 vs 4 vs 6) unclear or contradictory.
  - GSM / grammage not stated.
  - Photos on white background only; no glass / cutlery lifestyle context.
  - Pack composition not specified (identical or mixed colours).
  - Complaints about shrinkage after first wash and thin feel.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real GSM (target 180–220 g/m²) and confirm **pre-washed** status.
  - Muted neutral palette (natural / stone / off-white) — not the primary-colour trend.
  - Lifestyle photos: polished wine glass, ceramic vajilla, moka pot.
  - Include a hanging loop and a small kraft belly-band with a Spanish care card.
  - Honest listing angle: *"sin pelusa para cristalería y vajilla"*.
- **Suggested bundle / variation strategy:** Launch a single SKU = 6-pack, 50×70 cm, mixed neutrals, €22.90. Once reviews are stable, add a 3-pack starter at €13.90 and a "cocinero" gift-box 6-pack for Navidad.

### 2) Chenille Anti-Slip Bath Mat — 2-pack (small + medium)

- **Spanish keyword:** *alfombra de baño chenilla*, *alfombrilla baño antideslizante*, *alfombra baño absorbente*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Bath mats are a true replenishment SKU (stained, worn, replaced every 12–18 months). Chenille (long-loop microfibre or cotton chenille) with a non-slip TPR back is a well-understood category where existing Amazon.es listings compete mainly on colour count, not on quality signals. A 2-pack (large + small) solves the everyday reality that every bathroom needs one in front of the shower and one in front of the sink — most competitors sell single mats.
- **Demand signals:** Year-round steady replenishment; light spike in September (rentrée / new-flat season) and January (post-Reyes home refresh).
- **Competition signals:** Very fragmented — dozens of small private labels, most reviews clustered around 3.9–4.3 stars, price band €12–€25 for a single mat, €22–€38 for 2-packs. Realistic entry with 100–500 reviews barrier.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Non-slip backing peeling / disintegrating after 6–10 washes (very frequent 1-star complaint).
  - Shedding / lint on darker floors.
  - Colour photos differ from delivered mat (grey-beige mismatch).
  - Dimensions given only in one size in listings that show two.
  - Spanish copy machine-translated.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish TPR-back adhesion test (image showing mat + weight staying on tile).
  - Real GSM (target 1,200–1,500 g/m² for the chenille pile) and pre-washed confirmation.
  - 3 muted colours only (arena / salvia / off-white).
  - Clear pack composition: 1× 50×80 cm + 1× 40×60 cm, both same colour.
  - Care card in Spanish + hanging loop for line-drying.
- **Suggested bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as a **matched 2-pack (large + small, same colour)** at €26.90. Later add a 3-piece "bathroom set" (2-pack + toilet contour mat) once base SKU is profitable.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium**

### 3) Linen-Look Napkin Set — 12-pack (Servilletas de tela)

- **Spanish keyword:** *servilletas de tela*, *servilletas lino aspecto*, *servilletas reutilizables tela*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Textiles de mesa › Servilletas
- **Why worth researching:** Cloth napkins are a growing category on Amazon.es, driven by the "less-waste household" trend and by hosts who want a table that looks intentional. A 12-pack solves the pain point that most competitors sell 4- or 6-packs — not enough for a family lunch. Linen-look poly-cotton (or cotton / linen blend) photographs the same as pure linen but ships lighter and costs less, giving Rosanube margin room.
- **Demand signals:** Steady with peaks around Navidad, Reyes, weddings/comuniones season (May–June). Table-textile category on Amazon.es is thin outside the Christmas cluster.
- **Competition signals:** Present but shallow — the top of category is dominated by 2–3 small brands, most 6-packs at €14–€22. A 12-pack, muted-palette, Spanish-first listing is currently a gap.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fabric composition vague ("linen look") — buyers complain about polyester-heavy feel.
  - Napkins arrive creased; no note about ironing / steaming.
  - Colours photograph brighter than delivered.
  - Poor hem quality (fraying after 4–5 washes).
  - Pack size mismatch — reviews frequently say "arrived 4, expected 6".
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish exact composition (e.g. 55% linen / 45% cotton, or 100% cotton linen-look) and GSM.
  - Mitred corner + double-stitched hem shown in macro photo.
  - 45×45 cm standard size in Spanish market.
  - 3 muted colours (arena / salvia / blanco roto) — one SKU per colour.
  - Small kraft box with a Spanish care card — makes the 12-pack instantly gift-worthy.
- **Suggested bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as **12-pack single-colour** at €29.90. Later add a "mesa completa" bundle: 12 napkins + 6 placemats.

### 4) Microfibre Hair Turban Towel — 2-pack

- **Spanish keyword:** *turbante microfibra pelo*, *toalla pelo microfibra*, *turbante secado cabello*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Baño › Toallas › Toallas para el cabello (also indexes in Belleza)
- **Why worth researching:** A hair turban towel is a **very low-cube, low-weight** FBA SKU (single unit fits a small polybag <120 g). Non-seasonal replenishment. Amazon.es has a mid-density catalogue of options but almost all are either brand-dominated single-piece luxury (Aquis-style, €25+) **or** generic 2-packs at €8–€14 with poor Spanish copy and thin microfibre. There is a clear middle-tier gap: honest 350–400 g/m² microfibre 2-pack at €16–€22 with proper Spanish listing.
- **Demand signals:** Steady daily demand from the curly-hair / low-poo / long-hair communities; not seasonal; frequently gifted around Reyes and Día de la Madre.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented mid-tier; premium tier dominated by Aquis / Kitsch. Realistic review-entry barrier: 100–800 reviews for mid-price PL competitors.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Microfibre GSM unstated → complaints about "thin, doesn't absorb".
  - Button loop tears off after a few uses.
  - Colours look premium in the ad, look cheap on arrival.
  - No Spanish care/use card.
  - Complaints that the turban is too small for long/thick hair.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real microfibre GSM (target 380–450 g/m²) and split composition (80% polyester / 20% polyamide).
  - Reinforced button + elastic loop, shown in a macro photo.
  - Larger dimension option (65×25 cm) to fit long / thick hair.
  - 2-pack same colour (not the confusing "mixed colours" pack).
  - Kraft sleeve with Spanish care instructions (do not use fabric softener).
- **Suggested bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as a **2-pack, one colour per SKU** at €17.90. Later add a "cabello rizado" bundle (2 turbans + 1 microfibre-plopping T-shirt).

### 5) Reusable Bamboo / Cotton "Unpaper" Kitchen Cloth Roll — 20-sheet roll

- **Spanish keyword:** *rollo paños cocina reutilizables*, *paños bambú lavables*, *papel cocina reutilizable*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Textiles de cocina › Paños reutilizables (also indexes in Ecoproductos / Cocina sostenible)
- **Why worth researching:** A rolled 20-sheet reusable cloth (bamboo / cotton, popper-fastened onto an existing paper-towel holder) is a growing category tied to household waste-reduction. Currently thinly served on Amazon.es outside a couple of Anglo-import brands. Small FBA cube, high perceived value, natural bundle candidate.
- **Demand signals:** Growing but still niche — search volume rising steadily since 2024 across EU marketplaces; not seasonal; strong repeat-purchase potential once a household commits.
- **Competition signals:** Shallow — mostly UK / German brands with English-translated listings. Price band €14–€28 per roll. Review counts on Amazon.es competitors typically 40–400 → an entry window exists, **but** category depth is not yet proven — this is a Watchlist pick, not a rush-in.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fibre composition ambiguous — "bamboo" often actually viscose from bamboo.
  - Poppers detach after ~30 washes.
  - Sheets do not stay rolled after washing.
  - No Spanish description of use case (fits paper-towel holder).
  - Weak / hand-drawn cardboard packaging.

  - Publish accurate composition and GSM (do **not** claim "bamboo" if it is viscose-from-bamboo — say so).
  - Reinforced popper construction, and a diagram showing correct paper-towel-holder fit.
  - Include a printed cotton drying string and simple Spanish reuse guide.
  - Muted neutral colours only.
  - **No** unverified eco / organic claims — describe reusability plainly.
- **Suggested bundle / variation strategy:** Launch a 20-sheet roll at €19.90; add a 2-roll refill bundle at €34.90.

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Silk Pillowcase (funda de almohada seda 22 momme)

- **Reason for rejection:** Category dominated on Amazon.es by 2–3 known brands (Silksilky, Fishers Finery-style imports) with 2,000+ reviews at €18–€30. Race-to-the-bottom price pressure on new entrants, unclear "22 momme" verification pipeline for a new PL seller (customers demand OEKO-TEX + certificate — a compliance/claim landmine we cannot honestly make on day one). Colour-accuracy return risk is high.
- **Config-based reason:** Fails "Products dominated by big brands" and "Avoid claims unless proven (organic / OEKO-TEX / hypoallergenic)".

### B) Weighted Blanket (manta ponderada / manta pesada)

- **Reason for rejection:** Heavy (5–9 kg) → tips into Amazon FBA "large / heavy" tier with punishing fulfilment fees on a €30–€60 selling price. High return risk (weight-preference mismatch is a common 1-star driver). Slides into semi-medical claims (anxiety / sleep / autism) that Rosanube must not make. Multiple sizes needed (single / double / king) → variation complexity high.
- **Config-based reason:** Fails "very heavy products", "bulky products with high FBA/storage cost", "products with medical / therapeutic claim risk", and "products with too many sizes".

### C) Cotton Percale Duvet Cover Set — Cama 150 (funda nórdica 240×220)

- **Reason for rejection:** Bulky and cube-heavy → high FBA fulfilment fee. Sizing complexity is severe on Amazon.es (cama 90 / 105 / 135 / 150 / 180 — each is a different SKU with a fitted sheet / pillowcase set expectation). Established brands (Utopia, Amazon Basics, LEVITEX, etc.) hold the top slots at aggressive prices. Colour-mismatch and thread-count claim risk drive high returns.
- **Config-based reason:** Fails "bulky / high FBA cost", "too many sizes", "products dominated by big brands", "weak margin after VAT, FBA, PPC, returns".

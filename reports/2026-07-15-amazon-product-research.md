### 1) Cotton Kitchen Apron — Unisex, One-Size

- **Spanish keyword:** *delantal de cocina algodón*, *delantal cocina hombre mujer*, *delantal ajustable algodón*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Cocina y menaje › Textiles de cocina › Delantales
- **Why worth researching:** One-size adjustable aprons dodge the size-complexity problem that kills most apparel on Amazon FBA. The product is gift-friendly (fathers, hosts, cooking-class buyers), photographs well on a hook or model bust (no full-body model shoot needed), and has natural cross-sell with the waffle kitchen towel and future napkin SKU. It also sits inside Rosanube's brand-fit sweet spot: practical, home, textile, unbranded aesthetic.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Steady year-round; consistent presence in "textiles de cocina" and "regalos originales" search paths; visible uplift for *Día del padre* (March in Spain) and Navidad/Reyes. Not season-only.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. Two clusters: (a) cheap €7–€12 polyester aprons with printed slogans and (b) €18–€30 pseudo-artisan cotton/linen aprons. The middle band (€15–€22, honest cotton, adult neutral colours) is thin.
- **Price band (qualitative):** €12–€25 for a single well-photographed cotton apron.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fibre composition stated as "algodón" when it's cotton/polyester blend — common review complaint.
  - Straps too short for larger torsos (a repeated 1-star complaint on Amazon.es).
  - Single, poorly-styled product-on-white image; no lifestyle shot.
  - No pocket dimensions given (buyers ask if a phone / tablet fits).
  - Machine-translated Spanish bullet points.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Honest fibre label (100% cotton, canvas or twill; state GSM — target 240–320 g/m²).
  - **Extra-long straps** (adjustable neck loop + side ties long enough for waist 110+ cm) — call this out explicitly in the listing.
  - Two functional front pockets with stated dimensions.
  - Two colours only at launch (natural / off-white and salvia or terracotta) — no slogan prints.
  - Kraft belly-band packaging + Spanish care label → giftable without extra unit cost.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as single apron €18.90. Later create a **"Rosanube Cocina"** kraft-box bundle: 1 apron + 2 waffle kitchen towels (from the 2026-07-01 pipeline) at €29.90 — targets *Día del padre* and Navidad gift searches.

### 2) Cloth Napkin Set — 6-Pack, Cotton / Linen-Look

- **Spanish keyword:** *servilletas de tela algodón*, *servilletas tela pack 6*, *servilletas mesa lino*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Cocina y menaje › Textiles de mesa › Servilletas
- **Why worth researching:** Cloth napkins are boring, high-repeat, low-return, and — importantly — the natural companion SKU to the linen-look table runner shortlisted on 2026-07-01. They enable a "Mesa Rosanube" bundle without ever forcing Rosanube into the size-heavy tablecloth category. Small, flat, cheap FBA, easy to stack in variation trees (colour) without exploding the SKU count.
- **Demand signals:** Steady, with visible uplift around Navidad, Reyes, communions (May–June in Spain), and back-to-formal-dining seasons. Non-seasonal baseline is solid: many Spanish households replace stained napkins each year.
- **Competition signals:** Manageable and fragmented. Amazon Basics has a footprint, but the aesthetic-driven neutral-palette niche is dominated by small importers with weak photos and inconsistent quality. Review counts vary widely (30 – 3,000+), showing an achievable review-entry ladder.
- **Price band (qualitative):** €12–€24 for a 6-pack; €18–€35 for an 8–12-pack.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Colours in listing photos differ from delivered product (frequent complaint driver).
  - Fabric is often 100% polyester sold as "efecto lino" without stating it clearly.
  - Uneven hems and loose threads on first wash.
  - Sizes vary (40×40 vs 45×45 vs 50×50) with no clear size chart in Spanish.
  - No pressing/care instructions in Spanish.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Honest fibre disclosure (100% cotton, or clearly-labelled cotton/linen blend — never "linen-look polyester" pretending to be linen).
  - Mitred corners (small, visible quality signal — photograph it).
  - Uniform 45×45 cm size (Amazon.es Spanish dining standard) + neat kraft-band packaging.
  - **Palette matched to the linen-look table runner** in the pipeline → same "Mesa Rosanube" family.
  - Photograph on a real Spanish table (ceramic tapas plates, terracotta) — not the stock white-background 3D render.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch 6-pack in one colour (natural / cru). Add a second colourway (salvia or terracotta) after the first 50 reviews. Combine with linen-look runner into a Navidad kraft-box bundle at €34.90.

### 3) Swedish Reusable Dishcloths — "Bayetas Suecas" 8- or 10-Pack

- **Spanish keyword:** *bayetas suecas*, *bayetas reutilizables cocina*, *paños esponja celulosa algodón*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Limpieza › Bayetas y paños
- **Why worth researching:** Bayetas suecas (cellulose + cotton composite sponge cloths, ~17×20 cm) are the fastest-growing subcategory of Amazon.es kitchen cleaning textiles. They are ultra-light, ship flat, tiny FBA cost, high replenishment (buyers replace every 4–8 weeks), and align with the "reduce plastic / paper roll" narrative that Spanish households — especially urban, sustainability-aware buyers — are pulling on. Weak private-label positioning today on Amazon.es leaves aesthetic + honesty differentiation open.
- **Demand signals:** Steady and rising over the past 24 months on Amazon.es; consistently appears in "novedades" and "más vendidos" for the Bayetas subcategory. Non-seasonal, replenishment-driven.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. A few Nordic-import brands (Wettex-style) sit at the top; below them, dozens of Chinese-origin generics with printed patterns and low prices. The "clean, neutral, honest-composition" middle is thin.
- **Price band (qualitative):** €9–€18 for an 8–10-pack.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Compositions labelled "eco / biodegradable" without disclosing the exact cellulose/cotton ratio.
  - Overly bright, printed cartoon patterns that don't fit a clean home.
  - Packaging is thin plastic sleeve — undermines the "eco" story.
  - No clear guidance on how many washes / how long they last.
  - Sizes and quantities inconsistent between listing image and delivered pack.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Honest composition callout: state the cellulose/cotton % on the packaging and listing.
  - Compostable / plastic-free kraft packaging (verify with supplier, don't assume).
  - Muted neutral palette (2–3 colours), no cartoons, no slogans.
  - Include a small Spanish "how to use / cómo lavar" insert card (dishwasher-safe, top rack, up to X washes).
  - Sell as a **10-pack** (better price psychology than 8) at €12.90–€14.90.
  - **Do not** claim "antibacterial", "hypoallergenic" or "medical grade" — none provable without certificate (per config compliance rules).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Single 10-pack hero SKU. Later: cross-sell with the waffle kitchen towel and the apron into a "Rosanube Kitchen Starter" bundle. A refill-style subscription is not available on Amazon.es for this SKU tier — ignore.

### 4) Cotton / Chenille Bath Mat — Standard 50×80 cm (Non-Memory-Foam)

- **Spanish keyword:** *alfombra baño algodón*, *alfombrilla baño chenilla*, *alfombra baño antideslizante algodón*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** The memory-foam bath mat sub-niche was **rejected on 2026-07-01** (Amazon Basics dominance, odour/mould complaints, weak differentiation, "antibacterial" claim risk). The **cotton/chenille** sub-niche is a different animal: fragmented competition, higher price band, real fabric quality complaints Rosanube can solve, no medical-grade claim needed, and photograph much better in a "clean Iberian bathroom" scene. It also cross-sells naturally with the peshtemal and waffle bath towels already shortlisted.
- **Demand signals:** Steady year-round; small uplift each autumn (post-summer bathroom refresh) and in January (post-Reyes reset). Non-seasonal baseline is stable — a bath mat is a replacement purchase every 12–24 months.
- **Competition signals:** Manageable. Cotton bath mats are less brand-dominated than memory-foam. Many mid-tier listings sit at €14–€25 with 200–2,000 reviews; the top has room for a well-photographed private-label entry.
- **Price band (qualitative):** €14–€28 for a single 50×80 cm mat; €22–€38 for a 2-pack.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Anti-slip backing degrades or comes off in the wash — repeated 1-star driver.
  - Colours shift between listing and delivered product.
  - GSM undisclosed; buyers can't tell "thick and plush" from "thin and cheap" before purchase.
  - Sizes limited to 50×80 cm only; the 60×90 cm and 40×60 cm variants are underserved.
  - "Machine washable" claim without stating temperature or dryer safety.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish honest GSM (target 1,600–2,000 g/m² for chenille, or 700–900 g/m² for cotton loop).
  - Stitched-in latex/TPR anti-slip backing with documented wash-cycle rating.
  - Two sizes at launch: 50×80 cm and 60×90 cm (one colourway each).
  - Neutral palette matched to the peshtemal + waffle bath towel SKUs (same "bathroom capsule").
  - Care label with wash temperature, drying, and life-cycle guidance in Spanish.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch a single-mat SKU per size. Later: **"Bath Capsule" bundle** = 1 bath mat + 1 peshtemal (from prior report) for €39–€45.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** (anti-slip backing longevity is the main sample-stage risk)

### 5) Oven Mitt + Pot Holder Set — Cotton, Heat-Resistant

- **Spanish keyword:** *manopla horno algodón*, *manopla y agarrador cocina*, *guante horno pack*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Cocina y menaje › Textiles de cocina › Manoplas y agarradores
- **Why worth researching:** Small, light, cheap FBA, high replenishment (mitts get burned/stained and replaced), one-size, giftable, and a genuine complement to the apron + kitchen towel SKUs Rosanube is already lining up. Amazon.es is thin on aesthetically clean private-label entries — most listings are either cheap silicone (a different sub-niche) or cheap printed cotton with polyester filling. A quilted-cotton set with honest heat-resistance messaging is an open lane.
- **Demand signals:** Steady baseline; uplift at Navidad and *Día de la madre* (May). Non-seasonal replenishment behaviour is real.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. Amazon Basics is present but weak in aesthetics. Two clusters: (a) €6–€10 cheap Chinese sets, (b) €18–€28 aspirational sets. The €12–€18 clean-neutral middle is thin.
- **Price band (qualitative):** €10–€22 for a mitt + 1–2 pot holders set.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Heat resistance stated in vague terms ("resistente al calor") with no °C rating.

  - Reinforced hanging loop.
  - Length of the mitt: state cm from cuff to fingertip.
  - Palette matched to apron + kitchen towel line.

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Fitted Bed Sheet (*sábana bajera ajustable*)
- **Why rejected:** Kills SKU count — Spanish market requires at minimum 90, 105, 135, 150, 180 cm widths × two depths × 3–5 colours = 30–50 SKUs to compete. Amazon.es is anchored by Pikolin, Bassols, Amazon Basics, and El Corte Inglés-adjacent brands. High return risk from size/colour mismatches. Cash-flow-heavy for a first private-label launch. Per config: "avoid products with too many sizes, colours, or variations" and "avoid products dominated by big brands".

### B) Bath Robe / Bathrobe (*albornoz baño*)
- **Why rejected:** Multi-size apparel (S/M/L/XL/XXL × 2 genders × 3 colours = 30 SKUs), high return risk (fit, feel, colour), needs on-body model photography (expense excluded by config), and dominated by established textile brands on Amazon.es. Also carries the "fashion-adjacent" flag Rosanube's config asks to stay away from.

### C) Blackout Curtains (*cortinas opacas térmicas*)
- **Why rejected:** Very high size complexity (width × drop combinations), colour returns very high, "thermal / blackout" claims invite compliance scrutiny (energy claims), heavier packages push into higher FBA tiers, and the category is racing to the bottom on price. Weak brand fit for Rosanube's "home linen / kitchen / bath" positioning.

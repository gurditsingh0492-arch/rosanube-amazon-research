## Executive Summary

Today's five picks lean into Rosanube's core lane (kitchen, bath, bedroom, table textiles) but push slightly upmarket in **fabric quality** (linen, waffle, percale, chenille) rather than adding new categories. The rationale: Amazon.es competitors in these sub-niches are still dominated by generic polyester/blend listings with poor Spanish copy and vague grammage claims — a private-label seller who publishes real GSM, real fibre composition, and Spanish-native listings has room to enter.

Shared traits across the five picks:

- **Non-seasonal, replenishment-friendly** (with mild summer/gift uplifts, not seasonal-only).
- **Fragmented competition** — many private-label sellers in slot 5–30, few dominant brands in the exact sub-niche.
- **Weak existing listings**: no GSM, English-only colour names, machine-translated Spanish, weak photography.
- **€15–€35 target price band**, simple size/variant trees (1–4 SKUs max at launch).
- **Low compliance friction**: EU textile fibre labelling, care label, GPSR, EPR Spain — standard, no organic/hypoallergenic/medical claims.

**None of the five are recommended for a purchase order today.** All are proposed for **Further Research** or **Watchlist** pending Helium 10 Xray, Keepa 365-day charts, FBA Revenue Calculator screenshots, and supplier quotes.

### 1) 100% Linen Tea Towel Set (3-pack, natural / stonewashed)

- **Spanish keyword:** *paños de cocina lino*, *trapos cocina lino 100%*, *paño lino natural*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Textiles de cocina › Paños de cocina
- **Why worth researching:** Linen tea towels sit one shelf above the waffle/terry majority — same replenishment behaviour, higher perceived value, better margin. The Spanish "casa mediterránea / lino natural" aesthetic is well-understood and photographs beautifully without expensive model shots. Real 100% linen (not "linen look" polyester) is still under-represented at the €18–€28 pack price.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Consistent presence of *lino* keyword variants in the Textiles de cocina long tail; linen kitchen textiles seen as gift-appropriate for Reyes / Día de la Madre / bodas / estrenos de piso; secondary demand from home-baking and hostelería-casera segments.
- **Competition signals:** Present but fragmented — a mix of Baltic-origin small brands (LT/LV), Spanish resellers importing from India, and a handful of Zara Home / El Corte Inglés spillover in higher price bands. Amazon.es sub-niche is not brand-dominated at €18–€28 packs. Reviews often 50–800, entry barrier realistic.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - "Lino" claimed but composition is 55/45 cotton-linen or polyester-linen — reviews call this out.
  - No GSM stated (real linen tea towel: 180–240 g/m²).
  - Colour names in English only ("natural / oatmeal / sage").
  - Sizes inconsistent — customers can't tell if it's a tea towel, a hand towel, or a napkin.
  - Complaints about linting on the first 2–3 washes (normal for linen but not explained in the listing).
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish honest fibre composition (100% lino europeo or 100% lino indio — pick and document).
  - Publish real GSM and pre-washed / stonewashed status → softer feel out of the box, sets expectation.
  - "Iberian home" muted palette: natural, terracota apagado, salvia, blanco crudo — 4 max.
  - Spanish-native listing copy (not translated), care label in Spanish + Portuguese.
  - Explain the first-wash linting in the listing (customer education = fewer 1★ reviews).
  - Kraft belly-band with hanging loop already sewn.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as **3-pack mixed neutrals**, 50×70 cm, €22.90. Later add a solid-colour 3-pack (all-natural) at same price, and a "Navidad / anfitrión" 2-pack gift edition around €18.90.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium**
- **Score /50:**

  - Margin potential: 8/10 (higher AOV, still light + compact)

### 2) Cotton Waffle Short Kimono Bathrobe (Unisex, One-Size)

- **Spanish keyword:** *albornoz waffle algodón*, *albornoz corto kimono*, *bata baño waffle*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Baño › Albornoces
- **Why worth researching:** Natural expansion from Rosanube's core towel lane into the same **bath textile** shelf. A short kimono waffle robe (~90 cm) is lighter than a terry robe → cheaper FBA fees, faster drying (a real Spanish-apartment complaint about terry robes), and the same waffle aesthetic that photographs well. Kimono cut sidesteps the size-complexity trap: **one-size, belt-tied**.
- **Demand signals:** Steady year-round, mild uplift in verano (post-piscina / post-ducha) and Navidad (gifting). Guest-house / rural tourism buyers are a secondary segment.
- **Competition signals:** Present but not dominated — mix of Turkish-origin small brands and generic private labels. Price band €25–€45 for waffle short robes on Amazon.es. Review counts often 100–1,500; big terry brands (Sorema, Devota & Lomba) don't play strongly in waffle.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fit poorly explained — buyers don't know if "one-size" means M or XL.
  - GSM not stated (target 300–380 g/m² waffle).
  - Photos of a model only, no flat lay showing actual dimensions.
  - Belt loops fall out after a few washes (common review complaint).
  - No pocket, or pockets sewn too shallow.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real dimensions (chest, length, sleeve) and fit range in cm — "fits ES 38–48".
  - Reinforced belt loops + bar-tacked pocket bottoms (small documented change).
  - Muted palette: natural, salvia, gris humo, blanco crudo — 4 colours max.
  - Spanish listing copy + care label.
  - Flat-lay photo + hanging photo, plus one lifestyle shot in a Spanish bathroom (no model required if styling is strong).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as **single robe, one-size, 4 colours** at €32.90. Do **not** launch S/M/L variants at start — added complexity for marginal gain. Consider a "his+hers" 2-pack later at €59.90 if reviews confirm fit.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** (fit reviews are the main threat — mitigate with clear cm)
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 7/10
  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 8/10
  - Margin potential: 7/10 (higher AOV offsets slightly higher FBA vs. towel)
  - Risk: 6/10 (fit-return risk — needs Keepa return-rate proxy check)

### 3) Linen-Blend Table Runner (long, natural stonewashed)

- **Spanish keyword:** *camino de mesa lino*, *camino mesa comedor natural*, *runner mesa lino algodón*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Cocina y comedor › Textiles de mesa › Caminos de mesa
- **Why worth researching:** Table runners are a "one purchase, long life" item — low repeat, but very high perceived value per unit and photograph well without a model. Fragmented sub-niche on Amazon.es, dominated by generic polyester "linen look" runners priced €10–€18 that scream synthetic. A real 55/45 linen-cotton runner at €22–€28 has room to enter with honest photography.
- **Demand signals:** Steady year-round with a Navidad / Reyes / bodas uplift. Instagram-style Spanish home aesthetic drives search volume.
- **Competition signals:** Highly fragmented, no dominant brand in the €20–€30 band. Most sellers are drop-shipping generic AliExpress runners. Review counts often 30–600. Entry barrier low if photography and copy are strong.

### 4) Cotton Chenille Non-Slip Bath Mat (2-piece set, standard + contour)

- **Spanish keyword:** *alfombra baño algodón*, *alfombrilla baño antideslizante*, *alfombra baño chenilla 2 piezas*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Bath mats are a **replenishment SKU** (thrown out every 12–24 months for hygiene reasons) with real repeat behaviour. Cotton chenille is the fabric consumers actually want (soft underfoot, absorbent, washable) but Amazon.es is dominated by polyester microfiber mats with cheap PVC backing that lifts, curls, and molds. A real cotton chenille mat with a **latex non-slip backing** (not PVC) at €22–€30 for a 2-piece set is a clean differentiator.
- **Demand signals:** Consistent daily demand across the year; mild uplift in September (start of school year → home refresh) and January (post-Reyes home refresh).
- **Competition signals:** Present but fragmented; a few large bath brands (Sorema, Aquanova) anchor the €30+ single-mat band, but the 2-piece €22–€30 band is mostly small private labels. Review counts often 200–3,000, meaning entry is realistic but not trivial.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - PVC backing that fails within 6 months (curling, mold) — top review complaint.
  - GSM / fabric weight not stated.
  - "Anti-slip" claimed without explaining the backing material.
  - Contour piece dimensions unclear (does it fit a standard Spanish inodoro?).
  - Colours saturated in photos, arrive dull in reality.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Latex non-slip backing (documented, not PVC).
  - Real GSM (target 1,500–1,800 g/m² for chenille) — publishable, honest.
  - Standard sizes: 50×80 cm mat + 50×50 cm contour (fits Spanish bathrooms).
  - Muted palette: blanco crudo, gris piedra, salvia, arena — 4 colours.
  - Care label in Spanish, machine-washable at 30°C emphasised in the main image callout.
  - Spanish-native listing copy, close-up macro of the chenille pile.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **2-piece set only** (mat + contour) at €26.90 in 4 colours. Don't do a 3-piece set at launch — added carton complexity for marginal gain. Later add a matching hand-towel bundle at €39.90 if reviews confirm colour accuracy.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** (mold complaints if backing fails is a review-killer — sample quality check is critical)
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 8/10
  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 7/10
  - Margin potential: 6/10 (heavier + bulkier → higher FBA fee tier — verify with FBA calculator)
  - Risk: 6/10 (backing quality is the make-or-break)
  - **Total: 33/50**

### 5) Percale Cotton Pillowcase 2-pack (200 TC, standard Spanish sizes)

- **Spanish keyword:** *funda almohada algodón percal*, *funda almohada 45x110*, *fundas almohada 2 unidades*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Dormitorio › Fundas de almohada
- **Why worth researching:** Pillowcases are a **very high replenishment** SKU (staining, wear, hygiene). Sizes on Amazon.es are simple and standardised — mostly **45×85, 45×110, and 45×125 cm** (Spanish/Portuguese pillow sizes), which is a much lower complexity ladder than sábanas. Percale (200 thread count, matte finish) is the crisp everyday choice most Spanish buyers understand — a step above the shiny cheap poly-cotton majority.
- **Demand signals:** Consistent daily demand, not seasonal. Enters the replacement cycle roughly every 6–12 months per household. Repeat purchase confirmed by pack-of-2/pack-of-4 listings dominating the top of the sub-niche.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented in the €12–€20 2-pack band. Big bedding brands (Pikolin, Amazon Basics, Vivaraise) exist but sit in the €18–€30 band. Room for a private label at €14.90–€17.90 with honest percale claim.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - "Percal" or "algodón egipcio" claimed but composition is 50/50 poly-cotton — reviews call this out.
  - Thread count not stated or inflated (400+ TC claimed at €10 — implausible).
  - Envelope closure vs zipper not made clear; customers don't know which they're buying.
  - Shrinks after 2–3 washes — no pre-washed status stated.
  - Sizes shown in one system only; buyers with Portuguese pillows (50×70) get burned.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Honest 100% cotton percal, real 200 TC (published, not inflated).
  - Pre-washed / sanforised status stated → sets shrink expectation to <3%.
  - Envelope closure clearly shown in main image, no confusion.
  - Muted palette: blanco crudo, gris perla, salvia, azul niebla — 4 colours.
  - Size guide covers ES **and** PT pillow standards (45×85, 45×110, 45×125, 50×70).
  - Spanish + Portuguese care label.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **2-pack, 45×110 cm** (most common Spanish size) at €15.90 in 4 colours. Once reviews stabilise, add 45×85 and 45×125 as separate ASINs sharing photography. Consider a 4-pack "sábanas + 2 fundas" combined listing later.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low** (very light, cheap FBA, simple size ladder, well-understood category)
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 8/10
  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 6/10 (harder to stand out than linen or waffle)
  - Margin potential: 6/10 (low unit price offset by very low FBA fee)
  - Risk: 9/10 (low return, low complexity, low compliance risk)
  - **Total: 35/50**

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### DNP-1) Weighted Blanket (*manta con peso / manta pesada*)

- **Category:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Dormitorio › Mantas
- **Why rejected:**
  - **FBA cost breaks the model:** 5–9 kg product weight → Oversize/Heavy fee tier on Amazon.es. Margin evaporates.
  - **High return risk:** buyers return for "too heavy", "too hot in Spain", "smell chemical" — three failure modes.
  - **Marketing borderline medical/therapeutic** (*antiestrés*, *ansiedad*, *insomnio*) — violates config's compliance rules (see §Compliance Rules: avoid unverified therapeutic claims).
  - Dominated by TikTok-viral brands with heavy PPC budgets.
- **Decision:** **NO GO** — violates config §Products to Avoid (very heavy products, high return risk, therapeutic claim risk).

### DNP-2) Mulberry Silk Pillowcase (*funda almohada seda de morera*)

- **Category:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Dormitorio › Fundas de almohada
- **Why rejected:**
  - **Brand-dominated top of category:** Blissy, Slip, Kitsch — heavily reviewed, IP-protected brand names, aggressive PPC.
  - **Beauty/hair claims** (*antiarrugas*, *anti-frizz*, *hidratación piel*) — violates config's cosmetic/therapeutic claim restrictions.
  - **Momme count** (silk weight standard) is easily faked; reviews litigate authenticity constantly — high 1★ risk if supplier under-delivers.
  - Supplier verification for real 22-momme mulberry silk is expensive; 6A-grade certificates are frequently forged.
- **Decision:** **NO GO** — violates §Products to Avoid (brand-dominated, cosmetic claim risk, unclear certification).

### DNP-3) Bamboo Bedsheet Set (*juego sábanas bambú*)

- **Category:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Dormitorio › Juegos de sábanas
- **Why rejected:**
  - **Composition claim risk:** most "bamboo" sheets are viscose de bambú (rayon from bamboo) — EU labelling requires "viscose" not "bamboo" per Textile Regulation 1007/2011. Getting the label wrong is a compliance violation.
  - Frequent OEKO-TEX / GOTS / "hypoallergenic" claims used without valid certificates — direct conflict with config §Compliance Rules.
  - Sheet sets = high size complexity (135, 150, 180, 200 in ES + 90/105 solteros) → too many variations for a first launch.
  - Dominated by DTC brands (Kaydian, Zensations, Cariloha spillover) and Amazon Basics.
- **Decision:** **NO GO** — violates §Products to Avoid (too many variations, compliance risk, brand-dominated).

### 1) Cotton Dinner Napkin Set — 12-pack (Servilletas de tela)

- **Spanish keywords:** *servilletas de tela algodón*, *servilletas mesa lavables*, *servilletas tela pack 12*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Textiles de mesa › Servilletas
- **Why worth researching:** Cloth napkins ride a durable "reusable / less disposable" trend in Spanish households and hospitality-style dining. Small ticket per unit but the 12-pack format lifts AOV into the €18–€28 band, and the SKU is very light/flat → excellent FBA size tier. Repeat purchase driven by staining, wear, and set-expansion (buying more colours over time).
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Consistent presence in *Textiles de mesa* search results with several listings carrying 500–3,000 reviews; steady non-seasonal demand with predictable Q4 / Reyes / Comunión lift. Hospitality-adjacent buyers (small restaurants, rural houses / casas rurales) add B2B tail volume.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. Utopia Kitchen, Amazon Basics-style, and a long tail of private labels compete at €10–€22 for 12-packs. Few EU-brand household names dominate; entry review barrier realistic at ~100–300 reviews for page-1 competitiveness on long-tail terms.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Composition unclear ("cotton blend" without % split, or 100% polyester marketed as "linen look").
  - GSM never stated; napkins photographed only folded, hiding thinness.
  - Colour ranges skew primary/synthetic; no muted "Iberian home" palette.
  - Bad Spanish copy: literal machine-translation of English bullets.
  - Complaints: shrinkage after wash, colour bleeding, wrinkling, thin feel.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - 100% combed cotton, publish GSM (target 180–220 g/m²), mitred hem visible in image.
  - Muted palette: natural, salvia, terracotta, off-white, ash grey — sold as monochrome 12-packs.
  - Pre-washed / low-shrink claim only if supplier confirms in writing.
  - Real Spanish dining lifestyle photography (ceramic, wood table, olive/bread props).
  - Kraft belly-band packaging → giftable without extra unit cost.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch one SKU = **12-pack, 45×45 cm, one neutral colour**. Later add a 6-pack "starter" at ~€14.90 and a mixed-neutral 12-pack for €26.90.

- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Sábanas y fundas › Sábanas bajeras
- **Why worth researching:** Pure fitted sheet (**not** a full sheet set) keeps the variation tree small (2 sizes × 3–4 colours) while riding one of the largest replenishment categories on Amazon.es. Spain's dominant single mattress sizes (90 cm and 105 cm) are geographically distinctive — most global brands under-index on 105 cm, giving a Portugal/Spain-native seller an angle. Percale is the most trusted Spanish cotton finish and photographs cleanly.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Very high — sábanas bajeras is a top replenishment SKU. Many listings with 5,000–20,000 reviews at the mass end. Long tail below 500 reviews still moves units.
- **Competition signals:** **The most competitive of the five picks.** Bigger brands present (Pikolin Home, Today, Utopia Bedding-style, several Turkish importers). Review barrier is real. Bottom-of-market €7–€12 microfiber sheets are a race to the bottom — Rosanube must NOT play there. Opportunity is at the €18–€28 percale mid-tier.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Microfiber ("100% poliéster") marketed with cotton visuals — repeated complaint.
  - Elastic gives out after 10–20 washes.

  - Colour shifts significantly after first wash.

  - 100% cotton percale, declared **thread count (≥180 hilos)** and GSM.

  - Spain-specific size chart (90×190, 90×200, 105×190, 105×200) with fit diagram.
  - Muted palette + neat foldable pouch packaging.

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Faux-Fur Sofa Throw (*manta pelo sintético / pelo largo*)

- **Reason:** High return risk (shedding after wash is the #1 complaint in this niche), fashion-trend swing, colour-perception returns, and the segment is contested by aggressive polyester-only importers racing to €12–€18. Photography quality alone won't rescue the P&L. Also risks contradicting Rosanube's "clean, practical, comfortable" brand feel.

### B) Cooling Gel Mattress Topper (*topper viscoelástico gel refrescante*)

- **Reason:** Bulky and heavy → punishing FBA size tier + storage; high return risk (odour, firmness expectations); size-tree explosion (90 / 105 / 135 / 150 / 160 / 180 cm × two thicknesses); brand-dominated (Pikolin, Emma, Bedstory, Naturalex); implicit health/sleep claims flirt with the config's "medical/therapeutic" restriction. Poor first-launch fit.

### C) Silk / Satin Bed Sheet Set (*juego sábanas seda / satén*)

- **Reason:** Very high return risk (fit + slipperiness + colour mismatch complaints), brand-dominated at the premium end (Zimasilk, Slip-alikes) and race-to-the-bottom polyester satin at the cheap end, sensitive to laundering (durability complaints), needs model / bedroom photography investment, and the "seda" word triggers compliance scrutiny unless the product is genuinely mulberry silk (which the €25 imports are not). Not a Rosanube fit.

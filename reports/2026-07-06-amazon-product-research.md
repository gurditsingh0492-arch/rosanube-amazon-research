### 1) Cotton Waffle Bath Mat (2-pack, 50×80 cm)

- **Spanish keywords:** *alfombra de baño algodón waffle*, *alfombrilla baño panal*, *alfombra baño rizo alternativa*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Bath mats are a low-consideration, high-replacement purchase in Spanish apartments (mildew, staining). The category is dominated by cheap microfiber "memory foam" mats with recurring reviews about slipping and smell. A **cotton waffle** bath mat is a clean brand-fit angle for Rosanube: fast-drying, machine-washable at 60 °C, natural fibre — a real differentiator vs. microfiber without needing complex artwork.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — verify):** Bath mats sit permanently in Amazon.es Baño top results; non-seasonal, replenished 1–2× per year per household. Waffle sub-niche is smaller than terry but visibly growing on Amazon.es search suggestions.
- **Competition signals:** Terry / chenille / microfiber are saturated. Waffle is a **thin sub-niche** — 15–40 direct listings, review counts often 50–800, price band €14–€28 for a single 50×80 cm mat. Realistic review-barrier for a new entrant.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - GSM / grammage not stated; "cotton" claimed with polyester blend in the fine print.
  - No non-slip solution shown — reviews mention sliding on tile.
  - Photos on white background only, no bathroom context.
  - Sizes given ambiguously ("standard") instead of cm.
  - Complaints about fraying edges after 3–5 washes.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real GSM (target 550–650 g/m² waffle, 100 % cotton).
  - Add a **washable silicone anti-slip strip** on the backside (documented, not chemical).
  - Reinforced double-stitched hem to survive 60 °C washes.
  - Muted, "clean Iberian bathroom" palette (natural, arena, salvia, off-white).
  - Real bathroom lifestyle photos (Spanish tile, ceramic).
  - Kraft belly-band with Spanish care instructions + fibre composition.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch as a **2-pack** (mixed neutrals, 50×80 cm) at €24.90–€28.90 to hit the €15–€35 band per single-mat equivalent. Later add a single "grande" 60×90 cm SKU.

## Do Not Pursue Today

### A) Weighted Blanket (Manta con peso)
- **Category:** Ropa de cama / Salud y bienestar (categorisation itself is unclear)
- **Why rejected:**
  - **Heavy** (5–12 kg) → very high FBA fee tier + high shipping cost from Asia.
  - **High return risk** — buyers return for wrong weight, too hot, poor "feel".
  - **Compliance/medical claims risk** — the entire category leans on "anxiety relief / sleep" claims that Rosanube's config forbids without documentation.
  - Dominated by established brands (Levia, Gravity, Bedsure).
  - Doesn't fit Rosanube's "simple, practical, low-risk" positioning.

### B) Bamboo Bed Sheet Set (Sábanas de bambú)
- **Category:** Ropa de cama › Juegos de sábanas
- **Why rejected:**
  - Very high **size complexity** (90/105/135/150/160/180 cm × 2 depth options × colour variants) — Rosanube's config explicitly discourages products with too many variations.
  - "Bamboo" labelling is a **known EU/US regulatory pitfall** — most "bamboo" fabric is rayon/viscose from bamboo and cannot be labelled bamboo under EU textile rules. Legal risk under the config's compliance rules.
  - Sheet-set returns are among the highest in home textiles (fitted-sheet fit issues, colour, feel).
  - High PPC cost, dominated by AmazonBasics, Utopia Bedding, LaHome and similar.

### C) Round Beach Towel / Mandala Throw (Toalla playa redonda mandala)
- **Category:** Baño / Playa
- **Why rejected:**
  - **Seasonal** — mostly May–August demand on Amazon.es.
  - **Fashion-trend** dependent — the mandala print aesthetic has already peaked; classic sign the wave is fading.
  - Saturated on Amazon.es with hundreds of near-identical Asian-print SKUs, prices race to the bottom (€8–€15).
  - Sits against Rosanube's "not fashion-trend, not seasonal-only" rule.

## Executive Summary

Today's five picks stay inside Rosanube's core home / kitchen / bathroom / bedroom textile lane and share three properties:

- **Replenishment demand** (bed sheets, cleaning cloths, bath mats, napkins are consumed / stained / replaced — not one-and-done gifts).
- **Fragmented Amazon.es competition** with the top 30 slots dominated by generic private labels and small brands rather than a single incumbent — an entry lane exists.
- **Weak listing execution** by incumbents (English-only bullets, no GSM disclosure, unclear pack composition, unverified "eco / hypoalergénico / antibacterial" claims) — all levers Rosanube can pull without needing certificates it doesn't hold.

None of the five is a purchase-order GO today. All are proposed as **Further Research** or **Watchlist** pending Helium 10, Keepa, supplier quotes, and FBA Calculator screenshots.

### 1) Reusable "Unpaper" Kitchen Cloths — 6/8-pack waffle or bamboo cotton

- **Spanish keyword:** *bayetas reutilizables cocina*, *paños cocina reutilizables*, *rollo cocina reutilizable*, *toallas cocina lavables*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Textiles de cocina › Paños de cocina / Bayetas
- **Why worth researching:** A "sustainability-adjacent utility SKU" — buyers replace kitchen paper rolls with washable cloths. Repeat purchase every 6–12 months as they wear out. Very light + flat → excellent FBA fee tier. Photographs well without model shots. Aligns with Rosanube's "clean, practical, everyday" positioning without needing any organic / eco certificate claim.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to verify):** Steady non-seasonal search interest on Amazon.es, growing over 2024–2026 as Spanish households look at kitchen paper substitutes; several listings in the €12–€22 band have 4-figure review counts.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented — a mix of small "eco" private labels and generic Chinese-origin listings. Top-of-page listings often €10–€24 for 6–10 packs. Few EU-brand household names anchor the space.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Overused "eco" / "sostenible" / "bambú 100%" copy without any certificate or fibre breakdown.
  - Fibre composition either missing or inconsistent between title and bullets (a labelling / EU compliance risk for competitors, opportunity for Rosanube to be honest).
  - GSM / grammage never stated.
  - Photos in flat "white background" studio only — no kitchen-context shots to help the buyer picture use.
  - Pack composition unclear (mix of sizes? colours? all identical?).
  - Complaints about linting on the first wash, thin feel, colours bleeding.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - State real fibre composition (e.g. *80% algodón / 20% poliéster tejido waffle*) and real GSM (target 300–380 g/m²).
  - Pre-washed / shrinkage-tested claim — only if supplier confirms.
  - Neutral "Iberian home" palette (salvia, terracotta, ivory, natural) — no saturated primary colours.
  - Kitchen-context lifestyle photo (Spanish tile + wooden board + cast iron).
  - Do **not** claim *antibacteriano*, *hipoalergénico*, or *ecológico* unless a supplier certificate is on file (per config §Compliance Rules).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **1 SKU** = 8-pack mixed neutrals, ~30×30 cm. Later add a 6-pack "starter" at €12.90 and a bundle with waffle kitchen towels (SKU #1 from the 2026-07-01 shortlist) as a "Cocina Rosanube" kraft-box.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium**
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 7/10
  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 8/10
  - Margin potential: 7/10 (light, flat, low FBA fee tier)
  - Risk: 8/10 (low return risk, no compliance drama if we avoid unverified eco claims)

### 2) 100% Cotton Fitted Bed Sheet — deep pocket (sábana bajera algodón)

- **Spanish keyword:** *sábana bajera 100% algodón*, *sábana bajera ajustable*, *sábana ajustable algodón 150 / 135 / 90*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Dormitorio › Sábanas y fundas de almohada › Sábanas bajeras
- **Why worth researching:** A staple replenishment SKU. Standard Spanish mattress sizes (**90, 105, 135, 150, 180 cm**) are well-defined → the size complexity is manageable if Rosanube launches **one hero size first** (150 cm ajustable — the Spanish "cama de matrimonio" default) and adds sizes later. Non-fashion, non-seasonal, repeat purchase. Complements pillow protector (SKU #3 in the 2026-07-01 shortlist).
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to verify):** Consistent daily demand on Amazon.es; sábanas bajeras is one of the higher-volume sub-categories in Ropa de cama. Multiple mid-tier private labels with 2k–10k reviews.
- **Competition signals:** Mixed. A few larger EU bedding brands hold slot 1–3, but slots 5–40 are fragmented private label at €12–€28. Room to enter with clean listing + honest composition.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Composition claimed "100% algodón" but customer reviews describe polyester feel — trust gap.
  - Elastic band weak → sheet pops off after few washes (top review complaint).
  - Pocket depth (*altura del bolsillo*) not stated in cm → mattresses with topper don't fit.
  - Shrinkage after first wash — no pre-shrunk claim.
  - Colour photo vs. delivered colour mismatch (common in bedding).
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - State honest fibre composition + thread count (e.g. *100% algodón percal, 144 hilos*).
  - State pocket depth in cm on the front image (*bolsillo 30 cm — apto para colchón + topper*).
  - Reinforced 360° elastic (small documented change).
  - Muted 4-colour palette only (blanco, gris perla, arena, salvia) — no fashion-trend colours.
  - Spanish-language care label + composition label + size in cm.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **150×190/200 cm** as the hero SKU (most common Spanish double bed). Add 135×190 (single-plus) and 90×190 (single) once reviews stabilise. Later, cross-sell with pillow protector for a "Set cama Rosanube".
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** (return risk higher on bedding than on kitchen textiles — fit and colour complaints are common; also higher supplier-quality risk)
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 8/10
  - Competition: 5/10 (higher review barrier than kitchen textiles)
  - Differentiation: 7/10
  - Margin potential: 6/10 (heavier than kitchen textiles → higher FBA + freight)
  - Risk: 5/10 (fit / colour returns, quality perception risk)
  - **Total: 31/50**

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Electric Heated Blanket (*manta eléctrica*)
- **Why rejected:** Electronics + textile → outside Rosanube's core lane. Config §Products to Avoid explicitly excludes electronics. Requires CE/EMC/LVD certification, safety files, and thermal-fuse compliance. High return + safety risk (burns, overheating, faulty controllers) → high reputational risk for a young private label. Also dominated by established heating brands with 10k+ reviews and aggressive Prime Day pricing.

### B) Silk / Satin Fitted Bed Sheet (*sábana bajera seda / satén*)
- **Why rejected:** Attractive margin on paper, but customer expectations require documented mommie / OEKO-TEX / origin certificates that Rosanube cannot make without supplier proof (per config §Compliance Rules — no *seda* / *hipoalergénico* / *antibacterial* claim without a certificate). Very high return rate on colour, sheen, and fit complaints. Category increasingly anchored by beauty-adjacent brands with heavy PPC budgets. Poor first-launch fit.

### C) Extra-Large Beach Towel with Prints (*toalla playa XXL estampada*)
- **Why rejected:** Heavy seasonal spike (May–August only) with a long dead tail — poor fit with Rosanube's "steady non-seasonal replenishment" strategy and inventory-turnover risk. IP / licensing risk if the prints are recognisable (characters, football clubs, Disney) — config §Products to Avoid excludes licensed characters. Also brand-dominated (Benetton, Devota & Lomba, football clubs) at the top of *toalla playa XXL* results.

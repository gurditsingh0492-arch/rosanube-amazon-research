### 3. Pure Linen / Linen-Look Table Runners (Long Sizes)

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Amazon Category** | Home & Kitchen → Kitchen & Dining → Tableware → Table Linens → Table Runners |
| **Format** | 180 cm, 220 cm, 270 cm lengths × 33 cm wide |
| **Target ASP (UK)** | £16 – £28 |

**Why worth researching**
Table runners are a stable, low-return category. Most Amazon UK supply is short (140 cm) polyester or low-grade "linen-look" runners, leaving a gap for true cotton/linen blend runners in genuinely useful lengths for UK dining tables (which trend longer than US tables).

**Demand signals**
- Steady search volume year-round with predictable Christmas / Easter / wedding-season spikes.
- Sub-categories like "long table runner 270 cm" show very thin supply with strong sub-10,000 BSR on the listings that exist.
- Repeat purchase across colour palettes for seasonal swapping.

**Competition signals**
- Long-length segment is materially under-served — most top listings are 140–180 cm.
- Competition is fragmented; no dominant private-label brand has captured "modern minimal linen runner" on Amazon UK.
- Pricing range is wide (£8 cheap polyester vs £35+ pure linen), which means there's clear room for a £18–£25 sweet spot.

**Common listing weaknesses**
- "Linen" claim with no fibre breakdown — many are 100% polyester.
- Wrinkling complaints (genuine linen wrinkles; managing expectation is part of the listing job).
- Loose stitched hems and fraying edges.
- Lifestyle imagery often busy / over-styled rather than clean editorial.

**Differentiation ideas for Rosanube**
- Honest fibre callout: e.g. "55% linen / 45% cotton — naturally wrinkled, gets softer with every wash."
- Stone-washed finish so the linen looks lived-in from day one.
- Three lengths (180 / 220 / 270 cm) so it actually fits 6, 8, and 10-seater tables.
- Mitred-corner hem with care label tucked discreetly.
- Editorial photography on a real UK dining table, with placemats and napkins for context (cross-sell hook).

**Bundle / variation strategy**
- Runner + 4 matching linen napkins set.
- Runner + 2 placemats "tablescape starter".
- Solid-colour line first; introduce striped/jacquard SKU later if base sells.

**Risk:** Low–Medium (genuine linen sourcing adds cost variability; wrinkle complaints must be pre-empted in copy).

## Do Not Pursue

### 1. Weighted Blankets (cotton or bamboo)
- Heavy (5–9 kg) → expensive shipping and FBA storage.
- High return rate — buyers misjudge weight or temperature preference.
- Crowded by established brands (YnM, Mela, John Lewis) with deep review moats.
- Doesn't fit Rosanube's light/easy-ship profile.

### 2. Pure Silk Pillowcases
- Dominated by Slip, Blissy, and a handful of established premium players with £40–£90 ASPs and serious brand equity.
- Silk authenticity claims attract scrutiny and returns; Mulberry grade verification is a real cost.
- Differentiation window is narrow; competing on price destroys margins.

### 3. Memory-Foam Mattress Toppers
- Oversized, heavy, expensive to ship and store.
- Off-gassing complaints generate high refund rates.
- Categorically outside the textile/linen niche; better left to specialist sellers.

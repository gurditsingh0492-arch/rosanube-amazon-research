## Top 5 Product Opportunities

### 1) Chenille Tufted Bath Mat (alfombra de baño chenille)

- **Spanish keyword:** *alfombra baño chenille*, *alfombra baño antideslizante algodón*, *alfombra ducha absorbente*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Bath mats are a replenishment SKU (they wear, get stained, get replaced roughly every 12–24 months). Chenille tufted mats are the current "cosy premium" aesthetic on Amazon.es and photograph very well — an area where Rosanube's clean-neutral brand palette outperforms generic rainbow-colour Chinese listings. Small footprint (typical 50×80 cm), light weight, straightforward FBA size tier, and no complex variation tree.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Bath mats are a consistent top-100 subcategory in Baño year-round with a visible uplift each September (back-to-home) and January (new-year home refresh). Multiple listings show 4-figure review counts, indicating real, non-niche demand.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. The top page mixes small private labels, house-brand generics, and a few EU sellers. Common price band **€14–€28** for a single 50×80 cm mat, **€22–€38** for a 2-pack or larger 60×90. Amazon Basics is present in the wider bath-mat page but not dominant in the chenille sub-segment.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - GSM / pile density not stated; buyers cannot judge quality before buying.
  - Anti-slip backing described as "TPR" or "PVC" without explaining what the customer will feel.
  - Photos on white studio background — no lifestyle shot in a real Spanish bathroom.
  - Colour photos differ noticeably from delivered colour (frequent review complaint).
  - Complaints about backing peeling after 5–10 washes, and about fibres shedding onto floor.
  - Sizes given in cm but not visualised (no dimension diagram).
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real GSM (target 1,600–2,000 g/m² for chenille tufted) and stated pile height in mm.
  - Reinforced latex-dot anti-slip backing, described honestly (no "hospital-grade" or "medical" claims).
  - 3–4 muted colours only: cream, sand, sage, charcoal — no primary reds/blues.
  - Lifestyle photos in a real Spanish/Portuguese bathroom (marble or terrazzo tile).
  - Recyclable kraft belt with wash instructions in ES / PT / EN.
  - Small documented change: pre-washed to reduce first-wash lint (the #1 complaint).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **single 50×80 cm mat at €19.90**. If reviews stabilise, add a **2-piece set** (50×80 + smaller 45×45 contour) at **€29.90**. Avoid launching all sizes at once.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium**

  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 8/10
  - Margin potential: 7/10
  - Risk: 8/10 (low return risk, compact FBA)

### 2) Cotton Kitchen Apron with Adjustable Neck (delantal de cocina algodón)

- **Spanish keyword:** *delantal cocina algodón*, *delantal cocina mujer hombre*, *delantal ajustable con bolsillo*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Textiles de cocina › Delantales (also indexed in Ropa de hogar)
- **Why worth researching:** Aprons are a gift-friendly, low-variation textile SKU (one adult size, adjustable strap, no shoe/waist size trap). Amazon.es search for *delantal cocina* shows a mix of cheap €6–€10 imports and premium €25+ artisanal listings — a €16–€22 mid-band is thinly served with strong differentiation potential. Good Christmas / Reyes / Día del Padre / Día de la Madre gift use, so demand tail extends year-round.
- **Demand signals:** Cooking / grilling / baking hobbies are large on Amazon.es. Consistent presence of aprons in Textiles de cocina best-sellers; multiple listings with 500–3,000 reviews at various price points.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. Top page is mostly no-name generic or licensed "funny slogan" aprons (avoid IP risk). Very few clean, quality-first private-label brands owning the mid-price segment. Amazon Basics not visibly present in the top 20 for *delantal cocina algodón*.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fabric weight / GSM never disclosed — buyers get thin polyester when expecting cotton canvas.
  - Neck strap non-adjustable → sizing complaints.
  - No pocket, or one flimsy pocket that rips.
  - Cheap ties that come loose during cooking.
  - Photos with only one skin tone or gender, missing the "gender-neutral gift" angle.

- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Cotton canvas or cotton/linen blend, 220–260 g/m² stated on listing.

  - 3–4 neutrals: natural, terracotta, olive/sage, charcoal.
  - Gift-friendly kraft sleeve with a small care card in ES/PT/EN — perfect for the Christmas/Reyes gift window without seasonal-only risk.
  - Optional add-on later: matching kitchen towel — becomes the "cook's starter kit" bundle.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **single apron at €18.90**. Later add **apron + 2 waffle kitchen towels bundle at €29.90** — this ties into the July 1 kitchen-towel shortlist and creates a small "cocina Rosanube" mini-line.

### 3) Microfibre Hair-Drying Turban / Wrap (toalla turbante para pelo)

- **Spanish keyword:** *toalla turbante pelo*, *toalla secado pelo microfibra*, *turbante pelo mujer rizado*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Baño › Toallas (crosses into Belleza in some listings — stay in Baño for compliance simplicity)
- **Why worth researching:** A single-size accessory towel (one-size-fits-all with button/loop closure), tiny FBA footprint, low unit weight → excellent FBA fee ratio. Strong repeat-purchase behaviour among women with long / curly hair — a segment Amazon.es under-serves in Spanish. The "curly girl method" (método curly) community is active in Spain and searches specifically in Spanish for microfibre wraps and plopping towels.
- **Demand signals:** Consistent search volume in the hair-care / bathroom textile crossover. Steady year-round demand (not seasonal). Repeat purchase every 12–18 months.
- **Competition signals:** Top-page listings dominated by small brands, single-pack or 2-pack. Price band €7–€14 for 1-pack, **€14–€22 for 2-pack** (Rosanube's target). Amazon Basics not present in the top 20. Review counts commonly 500–3,000 — not a blank ocean but enterable.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Vague "super-absorbent" claims without a stated microfibre GSM.
  - Button loop reported as tearing off after a few washes.
  - Sizes stated only in inches or without cm — Spanish buyers complain.
  - Cheap, saturated colours (hot pink, neon) — no neutral option for adult buyers.
  - Poor Spanish copy; often literal translation of the Chinese title.
  - No dedicated "curly hair / método curly" positioning even though the search intent exists.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - 350–400 g/m² microfibre, stated on listing, with wick/absorption test photo.
  - Reinforced sewn-in button loop (documented reinforcement).
  - Muted palette: cream, sand, sage, charcoal — 4 colours max.
  - Spanish-first listing copy with a short "cómo usar" (how to use) section.
  - 2-pack from launch (increases AOV, reduces per-unit FBA overhead, matches the replenishment pattern).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **2-pack at €16.90** (mixed neutrals). Later a **3-pack "familia" at €22.90**. Optional add-on: matching microfibre face cloth 3-pack.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low** (small, light, cheap to sample and ship, low return risk if quality is honest)

### 4) Oversized Bath Sheet 100×180 cm (sábana de baño XXL algodón)

- **Spanish keyword:** *sábana de baño*, *toalla baño grande XXL*, *toalla ducha extra grande algodón*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Baño › Toallas de baño
- **Why worth researching:** The **standard bath towel size (~70×140 cm) is a repeated customer complaint** on Amazon.es — Spanish and international reviewers regularly write that the towel is "too small to wrap around" after showering. The 100×180 cm bath sheet solves that complaint directly and is under-served by private labels on Amazon.es (most large towels are sold as beach towels or bath sheets in premium-only €40+ listings). Sits at a differentiated size, not a differentiated fabric — an easier and more defensible angle than trying to out-GSM a saturated 70×140 market.
- **Demand signals:** Steady year-round demand for premium bath textiles. The XXL size query is smaller in volume than *toalla baño* but has less competition and much higher price tolerance.
- **Competition signals:** The 70×140 cm category is crowded (this is exactly why Rosanube should not enter it head-on today). The 100×180 XXL segment shows fewer listings, lower average review counts (150–1,500), and a higher price ceiling. A handful of premium EU brands (Vossen-style, Zara Home-adjacent) hold the €40+ shelf; the €25–€35 gap is thin.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Dimensions ambiguous: some listings advertise "extra grande" but ship 70×140 anyway (review complaint).
  - GSM not stated, or stated but not backed by any evidence.
  - Colour photography washed out.

## Do Not Pursue Today

### A) Fitted Bedsheets (sábanas bajeras ajustables)

- **Why rejected today:** Massive size complexity (90/105/135/150/160/180 cm × 190/200 cm × multiple pocket depths). Every SKU multiplies inventory and FBA storage cost. Return risk is high because customers order the wrong size or the wrong pocket depth and blame the seller. Amazon.es page is also crowded with strong EU brands (Amazon Basics, Utopia, Pikolin-adjacent house brands) and Spanish-market incumbents at low prices. Margin after PPC is thin.
- **Revisit conditions:** Only if Rosanube first proves inventory + PPC discipline on 2–3 simpler SKUs, and only entering with **one specific size** (probably 150×190 cm — the Spanish standard *cama de matrimonio*) — never the full range on day one.

### B) Duvet Cover Sets (fundas nórdicas con almohada)

- **Why rejected today:** Same size-explosion problem as fitted sheets, plus a pattern-choice problem (customers expect dozens of designs), plus higher return risk from colour/pattern mismatch with expectations, plus dominant incumbents. This is a mature category owned by brands that photograph the whole bed and run design/pattern collections — a capex Rosanube shouldn't take on before a proven hero SKU.
- **Revisit conditions:** Only after Rosanube has a stable hero towel/kitchen line, and only entering with **1 plain neutral SKU in 1 size** (never patterned, never a full set) as a controlled experiment.

### C) Weighted Blankets (mantas con peso)

- **Why rejected today:** Heavy unit (5–9 kg) → very high FBA fees on Amazon.es and freight-in cost from Asia is disproportionate to the €40–€70 retail price. Higher return risk (subjective "too heavy / too hot" complaints are frequent). Product also flirts with implicit medical/therapeutic claims (anxiety, sleep, sensory) — a compliance minefield under EU rules unless carefully written. Poor Rosanube brand fit today.
- **Revisit conditions:** Not before Rosanube has strong FBA logistics data on lighter SKUs, and only if Gurdit specifically wants to expand to bedroom accessories.

## Product 1 — Waffle-weave kitchen towel pack (4–6 pack)

**Amazon.es keyword:** *paños de cocina waffle / paños de cocina algodón gofre*
**Category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina → Cocina y menaje → Textiles de cocina → Paños de cocina

### Why worth researching
Waffle weave has moved from bath into kitchen textiles across Spanish homeware
retailers (Zara Home, El Corte Inglés Casa, Maisons du Monde) over the last
two seasons. On Amazon.es the sub-category is still dominated by flat-weave
generic terry and jacquard paños; a private-label waffle pack in muted
Mediterranean colours (sand, sage, terracotta, off-white) fills an obvious
aesthetic gap and photographs well. Product is light, small, easy to FBA, and
repeat-purchase friendly.

### Demand signals (qualitative)
- Steady year-round demand for kitchen towels on Amazon.es (staple).
- "Waffle" / "gofre" keyword rising in Spanish homeware search interest.
- Repeat-purchase behaviour typical of the category.
- **Needs manual export:** Helium 10 Xray for `paños de cocina waffle`,
  `paños de cocina gofre`, `paños cocina algodón`; Keepa 365-day on top 5 ASINs.

### Competition signals (qualitative)
- Top of search dominated by cheap flat-weave multi-packs (Amazon Basics,
  Vipalia, generic imports) at low price points.
- Few dedicated waffle listings; most that exist have weak imagery.
- Review counts on waffle-specific ASINs appear moderate rather than
  fortress-level — **Needs manual confirmation via Helium 10**.

### Common weaknesses in existing listings
- Flat, poorly-lit product-on-white photography.
- No lifestyle / kitchen-in-use imagery.
- Bullet points that just repeat "100% algodón absorbente".
- No clear GSM / weight disclosure.
- Titles stuffed with keywords, no Spanish shopper hook.
- Care/composition labels weak or missing on packaging shots.

### Differentiation ideas for Rosanube
- Curated Mediterranean palette (4 colours per pack instead of random).
- Explicit GSM and weave-density callout on listing and packaging band.
- Hanging loop with contrast stitching (small detail, visible in photo).
- Kraft/linen belly-band packaging = giftable and photographs premium.
- Spanish-language listing written for kitchen use-cases (secar vajilla,
  cubrir masa, tapar pan), not generic translation.

### Bundle / variation strategy
- Launch SKU 1: 4-pack, 4 colours (sand / sage / terracotta / cream).
- Launch SKU 2: 6-pack, monochrome (all cream OR all off-white) for bulk buyer.
- Later: 2-pack "starter" at lower price for review velocity, plus co-listed
  waffle hand-towel to cross-sell into Product 2 below.

### Risk level: **Low**

- Competition: 7/10
- Differentiation: 8/10
- Margin potential: 7/10

## Product 2 — Waffle cotton bath mat

**Amazon.es keyword:** *alfombra de baño waffle / alfombrilla baño algodón gofre*
**Category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina → Baño → Alfombras y alfombrillas de baño

### Why worth researching
The Amazon.es bath-mat category is heavily skewed to microfibre plush and
chenille mats. Cotton waffle mats — flat, quick-drying, machine-washable,
minimalist — are a distinct niche that matches a rising Spanish "Japandi /
Mediterranean minimal" aesthetic. Manageable size (typically 50×80 cm),
lightweight, easy to FBA, and low technical complexity.

### Demand signals (qualitative)
- Bath mats are an evergreen staple with predictable demand curves.
- Cotton waffle mats appear in fewer top-10 listings than plush mats, but the
  ones that exist show healthy review velocity — **Needs manual confirmation**.
- Cross-search overlap with "alfombra baño algodón" and "alfombrilla nórdica".

### Competition signals (qualitative)
- Category has established competitors on plush/microfibre (Vipalia, InterDesign,
  Pinzon, Amazon Basics).
- Cotton waffle sub-niche is thinner — a legitimate private-label wedge.
- **Needs manual export:** Xray on `alfombra baño waffle` and `alfombra baño
  algodón nordica`; Keepa on top 10 cotton-waffle ASINs.

### Common weaknesses in existing listings
- Poor photography — mat lying flat, no bathroom context.
- No dimension diagrams (Spanish buyers repeatedly ask about size in reviews).
- Missing GSM / weight, missing thickness.
- Non-slip backing not addressed (major review complaint on cotton mats).
- No wash instructions in Spanish on the listing.

### Differentiation ideas for Rosanube
- Silicone anti-slip dots on backing (directly answers the #1 cotton-mat
  complaint) — call it out visually in image 3 or 4.
- Two-size offering (50×80 standard + 60×90 large) — deliberate low variation
  count.
- Muted palette matched to Product 1's kitchen towels (cross-brand coherence).
- GSM callout (700–800 gsm) on listing hero image.

### Bundle / variation strategy
- Single-mat SKU (main).
- 2-mat "juego baño" bundle at slight discount (higher AOV, better ad efficiency).
- Colour-coordinated hand-towel add-on later (extends Rosanube bath line).

### Risk level: **Low to Medium**
Bath mats can attract "not as thick as expected" reviews; requires clear
listing expectations.

### Score /50
- Demand: 8/10
- Competition: 7/10
- Differentiation: 8/10
- Margin potential: 6/10
- Risk: 7/10
- **Total: 36 / 50**

## Product 3 — Linen dinner napkin set (6-pack or 8-pack)

**Amazon.es keyword:** *servilletas de lino / servilletas tela lino set*
**Category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina → Menaje de mesa → Textiles de mesa → Servilletas

### Why worth researching
Table linen — especially linen napkins — sits at the premium end of home
textiles and commands higher price per SKU with lower FBA fees per unit than
larger textiles. Amazon.es has a growing "mesa bonita" segment tied to
weekend meals, hosting, and giftable homeware. Linen napkins are simple,
non-fashion, and repeat-purchase for hosts.

### Demand signals (qualitative)
- Stable evergreen category with lifts around key gifting windows
  (Christmas, weddings, Mother's Day) — not purely seasonal, but with peaks.
- "Servilletas de lino" search has grown alongside the broader linen
  homeware trend.
- **Needs manual export:** Helium 10 Xray on `servilletas lino`,
  `servilletas tela`; Keepa 365-day on top ASINs to confirm non-seasonal base.

### Competition signals (qualitative)
- Category has a mix of small European brands (French / Portuguese linen) and
  generic imports.
- Room for a mid-priced Spanish-language listing between the €40+ boutique
  linen sets and the €10 polyester "linen-look" imports.
- Not obviously dominated by a single fortress brand — **Needs manual
  confirmation**.

### Common weaknesses in existing listings
- Many listings sell "linen look" (polyester or cotton-poly blend) but do not
  disclose composition clearly — customer complaints in reviews.
- Photography is often over-styled (foreign wedding tables) and does not
  connect with Spanish daily use.
- Confusing size descriptions (cm vs. inches, inconsistent per listing).
- Missing care instructions, no belly-band packaging.

### Differentiation ideas for Rosanube
- Honest composition: 100% pure linen OR clearly stated linen/cotton blend —
  no ambiguity.
- Two colour capsules: neutral (sand / white / stone) and warm (terracotta /
  sage / mustard).
- Belly-band gift packaging so single SKU works for retail AND gifting.
- Spanish-first listing copy, Spanish table-setting photography.

### Bundle / variation strategy
- Core SKU: 6-pack, single colour.
- Secondary: 8-pack "hosting" size.
- Cross-sell later: matching linen table runner (larger AOV, same supplier).

### Risk level: **Medium**
Linen sourcing quality varies widely — sample QC is critical. GSM and true
composition must be verified with lab test or supplier certificate.

### Score /50
- Demand: 6/10
- Competition: 7/10
- Differentiation: 8/10
- Margin potential: 8/10
- Risk: 6/10
- **Total: 35 / 50**

## Product 4 — Percale pillowcase 2-pack (standard 50×75 cm)

**Amazon.es keyword:** *fundas de almohada percal / fundas almohada algodón 50x75*
**Category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina → Dormitorio → Ropa de cama → Fundas de almohada

### Why worth researching
Pillowcases are the simplest, lowest-risk entry into bedroom textiles: only
one dominant size on Amazon.es (50×75 cm) and a handful of secondary sizes
(40×60, 45×95). Percale (crisp, matte, densely woven) is the format Spanish
hotel-style bedding buyers ask for. Repeat-purchase, giftable, low return risk
compared with full duvet sets.

### Demand signals (qualitative)
- Extremely stable evergreen demand — everyone owns pillowcases and replaces
  them.
- Percale-specific demand is a subset of overall pillowcase demand but growing
  as buyers distinguish it from sateen/microfibre.
- **Needs manual export:** Xray on `fundas almohada percal 50x75`; Keepa on
  top 5 ASINs.

### Competition signals (qualitative)
- Category is competitive at the low end (Amazon Basics, Utopia Bedding-style
  imports, various generic sellers).
- Mid-premium percale slot is less crowded and defensible with better fabric
  and better photography.
- Big brands (Pikolin, Belnou) focus on full sets rather than pillowcase-only
  packs.

### Common weaknesses in existing listings
- TC (thread count) claimed but not substantiated.
- Colour photos look flat (studio lighting), buyer complaints about "colour
  different from photo".
- Envelope closure vs. zipper closure not clearly explained on listing.
- Wrinkling and softness after first wash not addressed.
- Frequent "sizing wrong for my pillow" complaints — Spanish standard 50×75
  vs. imported 50×70 confusion.

### Differentiation ideas for Rosanube
- 200 or 300-TC percale, honest thread count, GSM disclosed.
- Envelope closure (no zipper) as a "no press points at night" story.
- Guaranteed 50×75 cm Spanish standard, with visible size callout on hero image.
- Colour range built around neutral bedroom palettes; matches optional duvet
  cover in a later phase.
- Recycled cotton polybag with EU labelling.

### Bundle / variation strategy
- Core SKU: 2-pack, one colour.
- Secondary: 4-pack (guest / travel).
- Later: matching pillow protector as attach SKU (same 50×75 size).

### Risk level: **Medium**
Bedroom textile is more competitive; margin protection depends on landed cost
and disciplined variation count.

### Score /50
- Demand: 8/10
- Competition: 5/10
- Differentiation: 7/10
- Margin potential: 7/10
- Risk: 6/10
- **Total: 33 / 50**

## Product 5 — Reusable cotton makeup remover pads (14–16 pack + laundry bag)

**Amazon.es keyword:** *discos desmaquillantes reutilizables / discos algodón lavables*
**Category / subcategory:** Belleza → Herramientas y accesorios de belleza → Accesorios / Hogar y cocina → Textiles

### Why worth researching
Small textile product with strong repeat-purchase mechanics (customers keep
adding sets), light and cheap to FBA, easy to bundle. Sustainability angle
is real and durable, not fashion-trend. Fits Rosanube's "practical textile
essentials" positioning as a small-basket entry SKU. NB: it lives at the
edge of the brand focus — treat as textile product, avoid cosmetic claims.

### Demand signals (qualitative)
- Steady demand for eco-swap products on Amazon.es; category expanded during
  and post the sustainability push.
- Repeat-purchase because buyers gift them and add sets.
- **Needs manual export:** Xray on `discos desmaquillantes reutilizables`
  and `discos algodón lavables`; Keepa on top ASINs to check for BSR
  stability post-hype.

### Competition signals (qualitative)
- Many low-priced generic packs; visible aesthetic differentiation is thin.
- Most listings copy the same bamboo/cotton language and packaging cues.
- Room to enter with clearly superior fabric (double-sided: waffle + terry)
  and better packaging.

### Common weaknesses in existing listings
- Fabric composition often unclear (bamboo viscose vs. cotton).
- Laundry bag included but described as flimsy in reviews.
- Pads too small (5 cm) — repeated complaint about coverage.
- Fraying after few washes — top negative review theme.
- No wash-cycle count claim.

### Differentiation ideas for Rosanube
- Two-sided pad: soft cotton flannel (make-up removal) + waffle (exfoliating
  rinse) — a real functional wedge.
- Overlocked / bound edges to solve fraying complaint.
- Larger 8 cm pad size.
- Sturdier drawstring laundry bag with visible mesh window.
- Kraft-tube packaging (no plastic pouch).

### Bundle / variation strategy
- Core SKU: 14-pack + laundry bag.
- Secondary: 16-pack "duo" (double-sided) with headband add-on later.
- No cosmetic claims on listing (no "removes makeup better", no
  "hypoallergenic") — stay compliant with `research_config.md` rules.

### Risk level: **Low**
Small, light, low return risk; category compliance load is manageable if no
cosmetic claims are made.

- Competition: 6/10
- Differentiation: 7/10
- Margin potential: 7/10

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### 1. Weighted blankets — *manta con peso terapéutica*
- **Reason:** Bulky, heavy (5–9 kg), oversized FBA tier destroys margin,
  medical / therapeutic implication risk (config forbids therapeutic claims
  without evidence), high return rate from "too heavy / too hot" complaints.
- Fails the "lightweight / easy-FBA / low compliance" test in
  `research_config.md`.

### 2. King-size duvet cover sets (juego funda nórdica 260×240) —
- **Reason:** Too many sizes (135, 150, 180, 200, 240), too many colour SKUs,
  competitive category dominated by established brands (Pikolin, Belnou,
  Utopia Bedding), high return risk due to sizing confusion between Spanish
  and imported dimensions, model / bedroom photography required. Fails
  variation-count and brand-dominance rules.

### 3. Anti-slip yoga / gym towel (toalla yoga antideslizante)
- **Reason:** Fitness-adjacent trend product (config says avoid trend
  dependence), competition dominated by fitness brands (Gaiam, Manduka,
  Amazon-listed athletic brands), aesthetic and use-case sit outside the
  Rosanube "home / kitchen / bath" brand promise, PPC-heavy segment
  (fitness keywords are expensive).

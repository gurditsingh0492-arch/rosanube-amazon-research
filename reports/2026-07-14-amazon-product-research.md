## Top 5 Product Opportunities

### 1) Cotton Chenille Bath Mat (single, ~50×80 cm)

- **Spanish keyword:** *alfombra baño algodón*, *alfombra baño chenilla*, *alfombrilla baño antideslizante algodón*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** On 2026-07-01 memory-foam bath mats were rejected (odour + slipping + brand dominance by Amazon Basics). Cotton chenille sits on the opposite side of the same category — machine-washable, natural fibre, less complaint-heavy, and matches the brand-neutral Rosanube aesthetic. Fits the "clean Iberian bathroom" story next to the future peshtemal / waffle bath towel launches.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Bath mats are a replenishment SKU (worn, stained, replaced ~yearly). Non-seasonal. *Alfombra baño algodón* returns hundreds of listings; multiple mid-tier ones show 3-figure to low-4-figure review counts — realistic entry point.
- **Competition signals:** Mixed. Amazon Basics anchors the low end (memory foam / PVC-backed). In the **cotton-only** slice, competition is more fragmented — mostly generic imports, few EU home-textile brands, price band roughly €12–€22 for a single 50×80 cm.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - GSM / pile height not stated; buyers can't tell fluffy from thin.
  - Complaints about slipping (backing missing or too thin).
  - Colour drift after 2–3 washes.
  - Sizes labelled inconsistently (50×80 vs 50×70 vs 45×75).
  - Single-photo listings; no lifestyle shot on real Spanish bathroom tile.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real GSM (target ~1,500–1,800 g/m² for chenille) and pile height in mm.
  - Latex-dot anti-slip backing (a documented, small, low-cost quality signal).
  - Muted palette matched to the future waffle-towel / peshtemal SKUs (natural, salvia, terracotta, off-white) — 4 colours max.
  - Care label + Spanish fibre composition + wash-temperature icon.
  - Kraft belly-band packaging, no plastic overwrap.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **one SKU** = 50×80 cm cotton chenille, single. Add a second size (60×100 cm) after ~50 reviews. Later, a "baño Rosanube" bundle: mat + 2 waffle hand towels.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium** (return risk is mostly aesthetic / colour accuracy → controllable with honest photography)
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 8/10
  - Competition: 6/10
  - Differentiation: 7/10
  - Margin potential: 7/10 (moderate FBA fee — mat is bulkier than a folded towel)
  - Risk: 8/10

### 2) Microfibre Kitchen Cleaning Cloth Pack (10-pack)

- **Spanish keyword:** *paños microfibra cocina*, *bayetas microfibra*, *paños limpieza microfibra pack*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Limpieza y hogar › Bayetas y paños de limpieza (crosses over to Textiles de cocina)
- **Why worth researching:** Pure utility, extremely high repeat-purchase, tiny FBA cube, and it directly complements the 2026-07-01 waffle-kitchen-towel SKU (same buyer, different job — cooking towel vs. cleaning cloth). Microfibre is where waffle cotton is *not* the right answer, so this is additive, not cannibalising.
- **Demand signals:** Consistent daily-life SKU. High review counts across dozens of listings, indicating steady sales for years.
- **Competition signals:** Very high volume of listings but very fragmented — many small private labels, no single brand controlling the category. Price band €6–€15 for 10–24 packs. The floor is low, so pack-size and quality perception matter more than absolute price.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Loose threads, colours bleeding on first wash.
  - Pack composition unclear (all one colour vs. mixed).
  - GSM claims (300 g/m², 400 g/m²) rarely proven.
  - Photography feels cheap and clinical; almost no "how it fits into a Spanish kitchen" storytelling.
  - Many listings smell of pure Chinese dropship — Spanish copy is machine-translated.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Colour-coded 10-pack (2× kitchen / 2× glass / 2× bath / 2× dust / 2× multi-purpose) with an insert card in Spanish explaining which colour is for what — solves a real customer confusion.
  - Real, tested GSM (target 300 g/m²), stated on packaging.
  - Cardboard sleeve, no plastic — differentiation vs. dropship polybag.
  - Zero exaggerated claims (no *antibacteriano*, no *elimina el 99% de bacterias* — per config compliance rules).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Single SKU = 10-pack colour-coded, €12.90 target price. Later a 20-pack "familia" for €19.90 and a slim 5-pack "cocina" version.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low** (tiny FBA cube, near-zero return risk, no compliance friction)
- **Score /50:**
  - Demand: 8/10
  - Competition: 5/10 (very saturated, but weak listings everywhere)
  - Differentiation: 7/10
  - Margin potential: 6/10 (low price ceiling; only works with disciplined COGS and small unit cube)

### 3) Absorbent Dish-Drying Mat (~40×46 cm, microfibre-back cotton top)

- **Spanish keyword:** *alfombrilla escurreplatos*, *tapete secado platos*, *alfombra escurridor cocina absorbente*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Cocina y menaje › Textiles de cocina › Alfombrillas de secado (currently thin sub-node)
- **Why worth researching:** This is a genuinely underserved sub-category on Amazon.es — the sub-node barely exists, most listings are US imports or dropship polybags, and Spanish apartment kitchens (small counters, limited drainer space) have a natural need. Simple SKU, no size variants beyond one big / one small.
- **Demand signals:** Search volume is smaller than towels but the top-3 listings hold their positions long-term, which usually indicates steady, unspiky demand. Category top-listings show only 100–800 reviews — very low review barrier.
- **Competition signals:** **Thin.** Almost no branded EU competition. Most listings are imported drying mats sold under generic ASINs. Price band €10–€18 for a single ~40×46 cm mat.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - "Absorbent" claimed with no GSM / thickness spec.
  - Curls at edges after 2–3 washes.

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Duvet Cover Set (*funda nórdica juego 3 piezas*)
- **Why rejected:** Sizing complexity is a killer — Amazon.es duvet covers need 90 / 105 / 135 / 150 / 180 cm variants **plus** matching pillow-case size trees. Category is anchored by strong EU brands (Pikolin Home, Sabanalia, Amazon Basics) with huge review moats. Return risk is high (colour, size mismatch with Spanish beds, fibre disappointment). Violates config's "avoid products with too many sizes, colours, or variations" rule and its "avoid products dominated by big brands" rule. Not a first-launch SKU for Rosanube.

### B) Licensed Character Kids' Bath Towel (*toalla infantil Disney / Bluey / Peppa*)
- **Why rejected:** Direct IP / licensing risk per config §"Products with strong IP/licensing risk" and §"Licensed character products unless legally licensed". Rosanube has no active license and licensing costs (per-unit royalty + minimum guarantee + brand approvals) crush margin for a first launch. Also brand-fit issue — Rosanube is a "clean, natural" home textile line, not a character-goods brand.

### C) Bamboo Bedsheet Set (*sábanas bambú*)
- **Why rejected:** Two independent problems. (1) Compliance / claims risk — EU textile labelling requires "viscose from bamboo" not "bamboo" as the fibre composition when it's regenerated cellulose; most Amazon.es listings mislabel this and face take-down risk. Per config §"Avoid claims unless proven" this SKU cannot honestly be marketed as "bamboo". (2) Return risk is elevated (customers report slipperiness, pilling, fibre disappointment). Combined with sizing complexity of the bedsheet category (see item A), it's a NO GO for a private-label first launch.

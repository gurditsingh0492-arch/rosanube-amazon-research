Fits Rosanube's "practical textile essential" positioning cleanly and is one of the few Beauty-adjacent SKUs that is actually a textile.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Consistent daily demand tied to the growing "zero-waste bathroom" trend in Spain; visible in top results year-round; not seasonal. Repeat 4-figure review counts across the top 20 listings suggest healthy category volume.

Review counts of the top-10 often range 500–5,000 — entry is realistic but PPC will be moderately competitive on the main keyword.

  - Composition unclear or inconsistent (bamboo / cotton / microfibre mixed without labelling).
  - Pads shed / pill after few washes (frequent complaint).
  - Laundry bag mesh too coarse — small pads escape and are lost in the drum.

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Electric Heated Blanket (*manta eléctrica*)
- **Why rejected:** Electronics + textile hybrid → **excluded by config** (avoid electronics, avoid batteries / powered items). Requires CE + LVD + EMC certification, GPSR responsible person, and product-safety file. Dominated by established heating-appliance brands (Imetec, Beurer, Daga) with 10k+ reviews. High return risk on any perceived defect. Not a viable Rosanube SKU regardless of margin.

- **Why rejected:** Colour-accuracy and "silk vs satin" complaints drive a very high return rate. Compliance-sensitive — customers expect momme / OEKO-TEX certificates that Rosanube cannot claim without documented certification (per config). Full bedsheet SKU also requires 5+ Spanish mattress sizes × 3+ colours = variation explosion, violating the config's "avoid too many sizes/colours" rule. Poor first-launch fit.

### C) Fitness Yoga Towel with Silicone Grip (*toalla yoga antideslizante*)

Seasonal spike Jan–Mar (new-year fitness), then flat. High return risk (grip performance is subjective, sweat / wash cycles degrade silicone). Weak Rosanube brand fit, no natural bundle with the rest of the lane.

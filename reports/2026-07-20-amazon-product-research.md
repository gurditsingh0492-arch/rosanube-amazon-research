### 2) Cotton Chenille Bath Mat — non-slip, machine-washable

- **Spanish keyword:** *alfombra baño algodón*, *alfombra baño chenilla*, *alfombrilla baño algodón antideslizante*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Baño › Alfombrillas de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Deliberate counterpoint to the memory-foam bath mat that was **rejected on 2026-07-01** (odour, mould, price race). Cotton chenille addresses the same customer need with a **natural material story**, is lighter (lower FBA), photographs premium, and has room in the €18–€32 tier where memory-foam sellers are absent. Natural fit with a future "bath capsule" alongside the peshtemal SKU already in the pipeline.
- **Demand signals:** Bath mats are a true replenishment SKU (worn, stained, replaced). Category is high-volume year-round on Amazon.es; the *algodón* / *chenilla* segment is underserved vs. the polyester-microfiber majority.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. A handful of generic private labels dominate €10–€16 polyester; the €18–€32 cotton tier has fewer listings and less-polished photography. Amazon Basics is present in microfiber but weaker in cotton chenille.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Non-slip backing peels or crumbles after a few washes — a recurring review complaint.
  - "Absorbente" claimed without a real absorbency demo or GSM.
  - Sizes shown inconsistently (some in cm, some in inches).
  - Colour photos don't match delivered colour — high return driver.
  - Backing labelled "PVC" or "látex" without EU compliance clarity.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - **Latex-free, TPR / recycled-rubber non-slip backing** documented on the label.
  - Publish honest GSM (target 1,600–1,900 g/m² for chenille) and dimensions **in cm, in Spanish**.
  - Two sizes: **50×80 cm** (standard) and **60×90 cm** (large).
  - Muted 3-colour palette matched to the future peshtemal SKU (natural, salvia, off-white).
  - Machine-washable up to 40 °C — clearly stated with a real wash-test image.
  - **Do not** claim *antibacterial* / *antimoho* without a certificate (per config compliance rules).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Single hero SKU: 50×80 cm chenille mat. Add 60×90 cm variant after reviews stabilise. Later: **bath mat + peshtemal 2-pack** as a "Baño Rosanube" starter capsule.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium**

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Fitted Sheet with Elastic (*sábana bajera ajustable*)
- **Why rejected:** Extremely high **size complexity** on Amazon.es (multiple mattress widths × two depths × colours = 20+ SKUs before you have real coverage). Very high wrong-size return rate. Category is anchored by mid-large brands (Pikolin house brands, Amazon Basics, Utopia Bedding) at aggressive prices, leaving thin margin for a private label without an established review base. Violates the config's "avoid products with too many sizes / colours / variations" rule. Not a suitable **first bedroom SKU** for Rosanube — better to enter bedroom via pillow protectors (already on pipeline) and duvet-adjacent items later.

### B) Bathrobe (*albornoz algodón*)
- **Why rejected:** Sizing complexity (S/M/L/XL/XXL) plus **gender-specific fit** issues → high return rate. Photography needs models (per config: "products needing expensive model photography" is on the avoid list). Category increasingly dominated by hotel-linen and department-store brands, plus high seasonal skew (Nov–Feb spike, weak summer). Fits the "cautious apparel expansion" band, but there is no data signal today that justifies overriding the config's caution. Revisit only with confirmed Helium 10 + Keepa data.

### C) Baby Muslin Swaddle / Blanket (*arrullo muselina bebé*, *manta muselina bebé*)
- **Why rejected:** **Baby / infant products** trigger enhanced EU compliance (EN 71 safety, choking-hazard labelling, additional GPSR file, potential Amazon category gating). Return / complaint rates in baby textile categories are unusually high (parents are sensitive to fibre origin, dye, softness). Reputational risk of a quality issue is disproportionate for a first launch. Violates the config's "products with unclear legal or safety restrictions" and "products with high customer complaint rates" rules. **Adult muslin is on today's pipeline (SKU #1) — the baby version is not.**

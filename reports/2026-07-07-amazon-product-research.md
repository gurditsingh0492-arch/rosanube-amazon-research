## Top 5 Product Opportunities

### 1) Cotton Bath Mat Set — 2-piece (rectangle + contour)

- **Spanish keyword:** *alfombra baño algodón*, *set alfombras baño 2 piezas*, *alfombrilla baño antideslizante algodón*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Bath mats are a replenishment SKU (they get grubby, they shrink, they replace). A cotton (not memory-foam) bath mat sidesteps the odour/mould complaint pattern that dominates the memory-foam sub-niche and lets Rosanube compete on honest fibre content and colour palette rather than on price. Selling as a **2-piece set** (50×80 rectangle + a curved contour "media luna" for by the toilet or basin) raises AOV and packaging efficiency in one move.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — to be verified):** Steady year-round. Repeat-purchase profile: buyers replace bath mats every 12–24 months. Not season-dependent.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. The top of the ranking is a mix of small private labels and Amazon Basics; few dominant EU household brands own the sub-category. Common price band €14–€28 for a 2-piece cotton set.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Fibre content vague ("100% cotton" written on listings that are visibly polyester in reviews).
  - Non-slip backing patchy — customers complain the mat slides on tile.
  - Shrinkage after first wash, colour bleed onto white tile grout.
  - Set composition unclear (you sometimes get 2 rectangles instead of 1 rect + 1 contour).
  - Only white background photography — no bathroom lifestyle context.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real GSM (target 1,600–2,000 g/m² for the pile face) and honest cotton %.
  - Reinforced dot-latex backing (documented, photographed).
  - Muted "clean Iberian bathroom" palette (arena, salvia, blanco roto, terracota) — no saturated primaries.
  - Bathroom-context lifestyle photos with Spanish tile.
  - Kraft belly-band packaging with Spanish care and fibre label.
  - Do **not** claim *antideslizante médico* or *antibacteriano* — plain "antideslizante" is fine and defensible.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch one SKU = 2-piece set, 50×80 + curved contour, 3 muted colours. Later add a 3-piece "family bathroom" pack (rectangle + contour + WC surround) once reviews stabilise.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium** (some FBA size risk for the 2-piece pack — must be measured, not assumed)

### 2) Percale Cotton Pillowcase 2-pack

- **Spanish keyword:** *fundas almohada percal algodón*, *fundas almohada 45x110*, *fundas almohada 45x85*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Dormitorio › Fundas de almohada
- **Why worth researching:** Percale (crisp, matte, breathable cotton) is the boringly-desirable Spanish bedroom fabric — cool for the Spanish summer, doesn't sound like plastic (a common complaint on microfiber), and doesn't carry the sheen/return-risk of satin. Selling as **just the pillowcase 2-pack** (not the full sheet set) sidesteps the biggest Rosanube risk in bedding: mattress-size complexity. Spain has only a small number of standard pillow sizes (45×85, 45×95, 45×110, 45×125), and the 45×110 case fits the very common 90 cm and 105 cm single beds.
- **Demand signals:** Consistent, non-seasonal, high replenishment (people buy spare pillowcases far more often than spare sheet sets).
- **Competition signals:** Present but fragmented. Big names sit at the top of the sheet-set ranking, but the "just pillowcase 2-pack" niche is dominated by small private labels and one-off importers. Price band €12–€22 for a 2-pack.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Thread count claims (200 hilos / 300 hilos) with no independent verification.
  - Sizing errors — the case is 5 cm short of what the buyer expected.
  - Zipper vs. envelope closure not made clear in the title.
  - Colour photos differ from delivered colour.
  - Complaints of pilling after 5–10 washes.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Honest thread-count disclosure and pre-washed status.
  - Envelope closure (no zipper failure risk), documented.
  - Muted palette matched to the Cotton Bath Mat SKU above — cross-listing photo bridges bathroom and bedroom.
  - Clear Spanish sizing note ("Compatible con almohadas 45×105 cm y 45×110 cm").
  - Kraft belly-band packaging with fibre label + care symbols + Spanish copy.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch 1 size × 2 colours = 45×110 cm. Add 45×85 as a second SKU only after the first is stable. Later, a 4-pack "guest room" bundle at higher AOV.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low**

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Electric Heated Blanket (*manta eléctrica*)
- **Why rejected:** Electrical product → CE/EMC/LVD certification, safety files, and higher liability. Category is dominated by a small number of established EU brands (Imetec, Beurer, Medisana) with decade-long review moats. Return-risk is elevated by cord/heat complaints. Violates the config's "avoid electronics / batteries / safety-sensitive" rule and Rosanube's brand fit (textiles, not appliances).

### B) Bamboo Bedsheet Set (*sábanas de bambú*)
- **Why rejected:** Fibre origin claim ("100% bambú" / "viscosa de bambú") requires supplier certification and EU labelling rigour (viscose ≠ bamboo in EU fibre law). Sizing complexity — bedsheet sets typically need 4+ mattress sizes × depths × colours, which explodes SKU count and returns. Buyers routinely complain about softness vs. expectation → return risk. Violates the config's "avoid unproven claims" and "avoid too many sizes/variations" rules.

### C) Character / Licensed Novelty Kitchen Towel (*paños cocina Disney / Marvel*)
- **Why rejected:** IP and licensing risk — the config explicitly forbids licensed character products without proper licence. Even if licensed, margin after licensor royalty is thin, and demand is fashion/season-driven rather than replenishment. Bad Rosanube brand fit (Rosanube is meant to feel clean/practical/premium, not licensed novelty).

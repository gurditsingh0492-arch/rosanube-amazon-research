## Top 5 Product Opportunities

### 1) Cotton Napkin Set — 6-pack or 12-pack (Servilletas de tela)

- **Spanish keywords:** *servilletas de tela algodón*, *servilletas de tela lavables*, *servilletas de mesa tela pack 12*
- **Amazon category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Ropa de mesa › Servilletas
- **Why worth researching:** Reusable cloth napkins sit at the intersection of three durable buyer motivations on Amazon.es — everyday table linen replenishment, dinner-party / hostess gifting, and the "reduce paper waste" eco angle. The SKU is small, light, cheap to ship, easy to bundle, and has clear repeat-purchase behaviour (people buy a second pack once the first is discoloured). Fits Rosanube's clean, practical brand exactly.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — verify with Helium 10):** Consistent presence in *Ropa de mesa* results year-round with predictable uplift in Nov–Dec (Navidad table setting) and April–May (comuniones). Multiple listings show 4-figure review counts, suggesting a real category — not a niche whim.
- **Competition signals:** Fragmented. A few mid-tier home-textile brands present but no single dominant player in the €12–€25 pack band. Common pack sizes are 6, 8, 12. Many listings look interchangeable — same white/beige photo, same fabric.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Pack composition unclear (solid colour × N or mixed neutrals × N?).
  - Fabric composition and GSM often missing; several list "polyester-cotton" without percentages.
  - Dimensions in inches or not stated clearly in cm.
  - Photos on white background only — no styled table setting in a Spanish home.
  - Complaints about wrinkling out of the wash, colour bleeding, and threads pulling at the hem edges.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - 100% cotton, stated GSM (target 180–220 g/m²), mitred / double-stitched hem — spell it out in the bullets.
  - Muted "Iberian table" palette (natural, terracotta, salvia, off-white) — not the saturated primaries competitors default to.
  - Real Spanish-home lifestyle photography (ceramic plates, olivewood, linen tablecloth undertone) — not a stock white studio.
  - Kraft belly-band packaging with washing / ironing instructions in Spanish/Portuguese/English — giftable without expensive gift-box tooling.
  - Include a Spanish-language "cuidado y lavado" insert.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch one SKU = 6-pack single neutral (start with natural / off-white), 45×45 cm. Add a 12-pack "hostess" version at ~€22–€26 once reviews stabilise. Later cross-bundle with the waffle kitchen towel from yesterday's shortlist for a "Rosanube kitchen starter" set.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low**

### 2) Reusable Cotton Makeup Remover Pads — 16-pack + laundry bag (Discos desmaquillantes lavables)

- **Spanish keywords:** *discos desmaquillantes lavables*, *almohadillas desmaquillantes reutilizables algodón*, *toallitas desmaquillantes lavables pack*
- **Amazon category / subcategory:** Belleza › Cuidado facial › Accesorios / Hogar y cocina › Baño (cross-listable)
- **Why worth researching:** Tiny, extremely light (small letter → small envelope FBA tier), zero fragile risk, growing eco / zero-waste buyer segment on Amazon.es. Cotton pads are a natural extension of Rosanube's bathroom textile identity, and the included laundry mesh bag creates a simple, hard-to-copy quality signal. Repeat purchase behaviour is genuine (they wear out, get stained, or get lost). Price point €12–€18 keeps blended margin strong even after Amazon fees.
- **Demand signals (qualitative — verify with Helium 10):** Steady all-year demand tied to daily skincare routine; light seasonality (uplift around New Year / spring skincare). Category is clearly established, not a fad.
- **Competition signals:** Very fragmented. Dozens of near-identical listings from small brands, few strong ones. Review counts vary widely (50 – 3,000+). Realistic entry with a differentiated pack.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - "Bamboo" claims that aren't verifiable — Rosanube can win by being honest with cotton + terry / cotton + microfibre and skipping unverifiable claims.
  - Laundry bag included but photographed poorly; often mesh tears in early reviews.
  - Poor Spanish copy; ingredient / material % often missing.
  - Colour options too many (rainbow packs) — read as gimmicky.
  - Complaints: pads fray at edges, colour transfers to face, poor stitching.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - 100% cotton, dual-face (terry side + soft muslin side), overlocked edges with stated thread count.
  - Muted 3-tone neutral pack (off-white / stone / clay) instead of rainbow.
  - Include a proper mesh laundry bag with a drawstring cord-lock — not a token pouch.
  - Spanish-language "cuidado" insert + a small usage tip card (how many washes, when to replace).
  - Recyclable card + kraft packaging, no plastic window.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch 16-pack + mesh bag at €14.90. Add a 24-pack "family" at €19.90 for repeat customers. Cross-list into *Baño* to catch bathroom textile buyers as well as *Cuidado facial*.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low**

  - Differentiation: 7/10
  - Margin potential: 8/10 (tiny FBA fee)

### 3) Waffle-Weave Kitchen Apron with Adjustable Neck (Delantal de cocina waffle algodón)

- **Spanish keywords:** *delantal cocina algodón*, *delantal waffle mujer hombre*, *delantal ajustable con bolsillo*
- **Amazon category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Textiles de cocina › Delantales
- **Why worth researching:** Direct brand extension of the waffle kitchen towel line proposed yesterday — shared fabric supplier, shared aesthetic, shared photography. Aprons are a real gifting / everyday SKU on Amazon.es (cooking, baking, hostess, Padres / Reyes gifting). Unisex sizing removes most size-return risk if the neck strap is truly adjustable. Waffle weave differentiates immediately from the sea of plain twill / canvas aprons and photographs beautifully.
- **Demand signals:** Year-round with predictable Christmas / Reyes / Padres uplift. Steady mid-volume category — not a mass mover but not a niche.
- **Competition signals:** Moderate. Dominated by generic canvas / twill aprons — waffle is a visible whitespace. Price band €12–€25 for a single apron, €22–€35 for a 2-pack.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - "One size fits all" claims with fixed straps that don't fit smaller / taller users.
  - Thin, non-absorbent fabric — the point of an apron is to protect and absorb; many fail on both.

  - Spanish care label + Rosanube woven label at the hem, not printed.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch single apron at €19.90. Add a "his + hers" 2-pack at ~€34.90 once reviews stabilise, and cross-bundle with waffle kitchen towels as a "kitchen essentials" set.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium** (very small size-return risk with truly adjustable straps)

### 4) Percale 200-Thread-Count Pillowcase Pair (Fundas de almohada percal 200 hilos, 2-pack)

- **Spanish keywords:** *fundas almohada percal algodón*, *funda almohada 50x80*, *funda almohada 90x45 percal*
- **Amazon category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Ropa de cama › Fundas de almohada
- **Why worth researching:** Pillowcases are the lowest-variation entry point into Amazon.es bedding — only 2–3 relevant Spanish sizes (**50×80 cm** standard, **45×90 cm** long, occasionally **40×60 cm**) versus the exploding size / colour matrix of duvet covers or fitted sheets. Percale 200TC is the classic "hotel-feel" fabric buyers know how to search for, avoiding the vague "silk-touch" satin trap. Sells year-round, high replenishment frequency (staining, yellowing, tearing), and low return risk since fit is unambiguous.
- **Demand signals:** Consistent year-round. Multiple 2-pack listings with 4-figure review counts.
- **Competition signals:** More competitive than the other four picks — bedding is a busier lane — but still fragmented across many small brands. Price band €9–€22 for a percale 2-pack in 50×80.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Advertised "algodón" that turns out to be poly-cotton — hidden in fine print.
  - Thread count claimed without any composition context.
  - Sizes cited in cm but not visualised; buyers regularly complain the delivered case is 2–3 cm off.
  - Poor envelope-closure construction (case pulls open in sleep).
  - Colour off vs. photo — a recurring bedding complaint on Amazon.es.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - 100% cotton percale, honest thread count (200TC is fine — don't exaggerate to 400).
  - Fully documented true dimensions with a size diagram in the A+ content.
  - Envelope closure with a 15 cm overlap — spelled out visually.
  - Muted 4-colour palette (white, stone, slate, terracotta) — not 12 fashion colours.
  - Care label + fibre composition label in Spanish, sewn in the seam.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **only** 50×80 cm in white and stone as two SKUs. Add 45×90 cm only after both stabilise. Avoid duvet cover / fitted sheet expansion until pillowcase demand is proven and reviewed.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** (busier lane, colour-accuracy risk, higher supplier-quality dependence)

### 5) Cotton Chenille Non-Slip Bath Mat (Alfombra de baño algodón antideslizante)

- **Spanish keywords:** *alfombra baño algodón*, *alfombra baño antideslizante algodón chenille*, *alfombra ducha absorbente*
- **Amazon category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** Yesterday's report rejected memory-foam bath mats (durability + hygiene + odour complaints). Cotton chenille is the honest opposite bet — soft, absorbent, machine-washable, and answers the recurring complaint that cheap foam mats stink after 4 weeks. Rosanube's brand fit is direct, the SKU is single-size / single-shape, and the FBA weight tier is manageable if kept under 60×90 cm.
- **Demand signals:** Steady year-round; small uplift in summer (rental / holiday-home restocking).
- **Competition signals:** Moderate. Memory-foam mats dominate, but cotton chenille has a growing sub-niche. Price band €18–€35 for a 60×90 cm.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Anti-slip backing peels off after several washes — a very frequent 1-star complaint.
  - Colour bleeding on first wash.
  - Weight (density / GSM) not stated — buyers can't tell if it's substantial or thin.
  - "Cotton" mats that are actually microfibre — trust broken on delivery.
  - Sizes listed in imperial or ambiguously.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - 100% cotton chenille pile (stated density, e.g., 1,800 g/m²), TPR anti-slip backing with a warranty statement.
  - Pre-wash + colour-fastness test documented in bullets.
  - Muted 3-colour palette (off-white, stone, terracotta) — no bright turquoise.
  - Clean Spanish label with cm dimensions and care instructions.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **one** SKU only: 60×90 cm off-white. Only after positive review velocity, add a smaller 50×70 cm and a stone colour. Avoid a 2-pack until FBA weight economics are verified — two cotton mats may cross a fee tier.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Medium** (weight / FBA fee tier + anti-slip complaints)

  - Differentiation: 7/10

## Do Not Pursue Today

### A) Duvet cover set (Funda nórdica juego)
- **Reason:** Explodes into a size × colour matrix — 90 cm, 105 cm, 135 cm, 150 cm, 180 cm, 200 cm × N colours, each requiring its own SKU, packaging, and inventory bet. High return risk from fit / colour mismatch, heavy FBA tier, dominated by mid-tier bedding brands (Pikolin Home, Estelia, Amazon Basics). Not a first-launch SKU for Rosanube.
- **Decision:** NO GO for now — revisit only after pillowcase (pick #4) proves the category and a supplier is qualified.

### B) Heated / electric throw blanket (Manta eléctrica)
- **Reason:** Electronics + textile hybrid. Requires CE + LVD + EMC + WEEE registration in Spain, higher return risk (electrical faults, thermostat complaints), oversized FBA, seasonal demand (Nov–Feb only), and dominated by established brands (Imetec, Beurer, Daga). Contradicts multiple explicit "avoid" lines in `research_config.md` (electronics, seasonal, big-brand-dominated, compliance-heavy).
- **Decision:** Rejected.

### C) Silk-look / satin fitted sheet (Sábana bajera satén)
- **Reason:** All the size-matrix pain of duvet covers *plus* fabric perception risk — "satin" buyers regularly complain the product feels synthetic, slippery, and sizes run small. High return risk, colour-accuracy risk, and the "silk-touch" claim is a trust minefield unless composition is documented. Weak Rosanube brand fit versus honest cotton bedding.
- **Decision:** Rejected.

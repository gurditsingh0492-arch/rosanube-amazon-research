### 2) 100% Cotton Chenille Bath Mat (non-slip backing, machine-washable)

- **Spanish keywords:** *alfombra baño algodón*, *alfombrilla baño chenilla*, *alfombra baño antideslizante lavable*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Baño › Alfombras de baño
- **Why worth researching:** A **cotton** chenille bath mat directly addresses the top three complaints on the *memory-foam* bath mats we rejected on 2026-07-01: (a) chemical smell out of the polybag, (b) mould after weeks in a damp bathroom, (c) can't machine-wash. Cotton chenille washes cleanly, dries fully, and reads "premium apartment" rather than "cheap hotel". Sits alongside the peshtemal in the bath sub-brand and is a natural bundle candidate.
- **Demand signals:** Bath mats are a stable, replenishment category — replaced every 12–24 months, or when moving apartment. Steady Amazon.es search volume all year; no strong seasonality.
- **Competition signals:** Two-tier market — one tier is Amazon Basics + generic microfiber at €8–€14, the other is chenille / cotton loop at €18–€30 with weaker photography and thinner review counts. The **cotton tier** is where Rosanube can enter without a race-to-the-bottom price war.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - "Anti-slip" backing peels or degrades after a few washes.
  - Colour bleeding on first wash.
  - Bulk / thickness overstated in photos; product arrives flatter than shown.
  - Sheds heavily in the first weeks.
  - Sizing shown in inches or unclear cm.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Publish real cotton weight (target 1,200–1,600 g/m² pile) and stated size in cm.
  - Latex / TPR backing rated for machine wash at 30–40 °C — state the number of wash cycles it survives.
  - **Pre-washed / low-shed** disclosure in the listing.
  - Neutral palette matched to the Rosanube bath range (natural, off-white, salvia, sand, charcoal) — 4 colours max.
  - Bathroom-context lifestyle photo, not a white background.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch 1 size (50×80 cm), 4 colours. Later add a **contour mat + main mat 2-piece** and a **bath mat + peshtemal** 2-piece bundle in a kraft belly-band.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium** (backing durability is the one review-risk lever)

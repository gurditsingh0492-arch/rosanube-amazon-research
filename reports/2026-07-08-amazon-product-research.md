## Top 5 Product Opportunities

### 1) Swedish-Style Reusable Dishcloth Multi-Pack — *Bayetas suecas / paños cocina reutilizables*

- **Product name:** Rosanube Swedish Dishcloth 8-Pack (cellulose + cotton blend, printed and plain mix)
- **Amazon category / subcategory:** Hogar y cocina → Cocina y menaje → Utensilios de cocina → Bayetas y paños / also indexes into Hogar y cocina → Limpieza
- **Why worth researching:**
  - Sits at the intersection of "kitchen textile" and "eco-friendly household" — a growing durable trend on Amazon.es, not a fashion spike.
  - Very light, flat-pack, cheap freight from EU/Turkey/India suppliers, ideal for small-parcel FBA tier.
  - Repeat-purchase behaviour is natural (cloths wear out in 2–4 months).
  - Existing top listings are mostly generic Chinese brands with poor Spanish copy and photo-on-white-background only.
- **Demand signals (directional, needs Helium 10 confirmation):**
  - Consistent long-tail Spanish keywords: *bayetas suecas*, *paños cocina reutilizables*, *bayetas ecológicas*, *paños absorbentes cocina*.
  - Sustained interest year-round (not seasonal), rising along with "menos plástico" household search behaviour.
- **Competition signals:**
  - Fragmented: many sub-1,000-review generic listings; a handful of eco-brand Nordic entrants (Wettex, Ecovibe) but not category-dominant on Amazon.es.
  - Price band roughly €10–€22 for 6–10 packs.
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Machine-translated Spanish titles ("*paño cocina sueco*" without diacritics or Spanish phrasing).
  - Cluttered novelty prints that limit gift/decor appeal.
  - No clear GSM/absorbency spec, no dishwasher/washing-machine wash-cycle count claim.
  - Weak A+ content, no lifestyle photography.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - Curated neutral palette (linen white, sand, sage, terracotta) matching Rosanube's clean brand aesthetic.
  - Print half plain / half tonal geometric — avoid loud novelty.
  - Clearly stated dishwasher-safe up to X cycles and machine-wash cycle count.
  - Spanish-native listing copy with EU disposal note (compostable end-of-life).
  - Sturdier kraft packaging as a giftable set.
- **Bundle / variation strategy:**
  - Core SKU: 8-pack neutral tones. Secondary SKU: 12-pack mixed. Later: cross-bundle with waffle kitchen towel (product #1 from 2026-07-01 report).
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium** (main risk: race-to-bottom on price if a large brand enters; margin per unit is thin unless bundled).

## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Fitted Sheet — *Sábana bajera ajustable*
- **Reason:** Very high SKU complexity — Amazon.es needs at least 5 mattress sizes (90/105/135/150/180 cm) × 4+ mattress depths × 4+ colours. Inventory risk is enormous for a first-time private-label seller. Return rate is elevated (elastic size mismatch). Dominated by Amazon Basics, Utopia, Bedsure, Pikolin. Fails the config's "avoid too many variations" and "avoid brand-dominated" rules.
- **Config match:** Products to Avoid — "Products with too many sizes, colours, or variations" + "Products dominated by big brands."
- **Status:** Rejected.

### B) Oversized Beach / Pool Towel — *Toalla de playa XL / microfibra*
- **Reason:** Seasonal spike from May–August then falling BSR; Rosanube's config specifically flags "Not too seasonal" as a criterion. Also brand-crowded (Boho, Amilova, Rainleaf), high PPC costs during the exact months you'd rely on for volume. Return-risk moderate (colour-bleed complaints). Poor fit for a steady-demand brand.
- **Config match:** Products to Avoid — "Products depending mainly on seasonal demand" + "brand-dominated."
- **Status:** Rejected.

### C) Generic Microfibre Cleaning Cloth Pack — *Bayetas microfibra multiuso*
- **Reason:** Sub-€10 selling price is below Rosanube's ideal band. Race-to-bottom pricing among IKEA, Vileda, Amazon Basics, and dozens of white-label 20-packs. Margin destroyed by FBA fee ratio at these price points; PPC unprofitable. Note: this is distinct from **Swedish dishcloth (#1)** — the microfibre generic is a commodity, the Swedish cellulose+cotton is an eco/premium subcategory with pricing headroom.
- **Config match:** Products to Avoid — "Products with very low differentiation opportunity" + "under €10 unless pack/bundle economics are excellent."
- **Status:** Rejected.

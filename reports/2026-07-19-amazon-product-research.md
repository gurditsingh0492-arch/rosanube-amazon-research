## Do Not Pursue (Rejected Today)

### A) Electric Heated Throw / Manta Eléctrica (*manta eléctrica*)
- **Why rejected:** Contains electronics + heating element → automatic disqualifier under config §"Products to Avoid" (electronics, dangerous-goods risk). Requires CE + LVD + EMC certification. High return risk from safety complaints, plug-format issues, and thermostat failures. Dominated by established EU brands (Imetec, Beurer, Medisana) with 10k+ reviews. Winter-only demand — fails the "not too seasonal" filter. No Rosanube-fit differentiation lever.

### B) Latex / Memory-Foam Mattress Topper (*topper colchón látex*, *topper viscoelástico*)
- **Why rejected:** Bulky, heavy, oversized → very high FBA size tier and freight cost. High return risk (buyers change their mind after unboxing; toppers are hard to re-fold once vacuum-seal is broken). Amazon.es sub-category dominated by a handful of established bedding brands (Naturalex, Duérmete Online) with strong review moats. Buyers often expect certificates (OEKO-TEX, CertiPUR) that Rosanube cannot claim without proof (config §Compliance). Fails config's "avoid oversized / bulky / heavy" rule.

### C) Silk Sleep Mask (*antifaz seda dormir*)
- **Why rejected:** Sub-niche dominated by beauty-adjacent brands (Slip, Blissy imitators) that market on momme count and OEKO-TEX / mulberry-silk certificates. Rosanube cannot make silk-origin or momme claims without documented certificates (config §Compliance). High return / complaint rate on authenticity ("this isn't real silk"). Very small unit → low absolute margin per SKU even at good percentage. Outside Rosanube's home-textile lane — better as a Watchlist item only.

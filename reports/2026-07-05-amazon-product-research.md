### 2) Microfibre Dish-Drying Mat for Kitchen Counter (Tapete escurridor platos)

- **Spanish keywords:** *tapete escurridor platos microfibra*, *alfombrilla secado platos cocina*, *tapete cocina absorbente enrollable*
- **Category / Subcategory:** Hogar y cocina › Ropa de hogar › Textiles de cocina › Paños y bayetas de cocina (some competitors sit under *Utensilios de cocina › Escurridores*, which is a listing-strategy opportunity)
- **Why worth researching:** A dish-drying mat is a **quiet replenishment item** — customers replace it every 6–18 months when it starts to smell or stain. Small pack, light, extremely FBA-friendly, and the category is under-branded on Amazon.es compared to the US/DE. Rosanube can enter with one hero size (40×46 cm) and win on materials and Spanish listing quality.
- **Demand signals:** Steady daily sales, non-seasonal; frequently bought-together with dish soap, sink caddies, and microfiber cloths.
- **Competition signals:** Highly fragmented — mostly generic PL sellers €7–€14 for a single mat; a few 2-packs at €13–€20. Few dominant brands, so review moats are low (100–1,500 in the middle).
- **Common weaknesses in existing listings:**
  - Complaints about mould/smell after few weeks (poor drying, cheap PU backing).
  - "Microfibra" claimed but composition not stated (often 80/20 poly/nylon undisclosed).
  - Sizes ambiguous or listed in inches only.
  - No lifestyle photo — just a folded mat on white background.
  - Poor Spanish copy; instructions on how to wash and dry missing.
- **Differentiation ideas for Rosanube:**
  - State composition + GSM + edge stitching.
  - **Two-layer construction:** absorbent microfibre top + quick-dry mesh underside (documented, not a marketing gimmick).
  - Machine-washable up to 60 °C confirmed on the listing; include hanging loop.
  - Two sizes only: **40×46 cm** and **40×60 cm** — clearly named in Spanish (S / L).
  - 3 muted colours only (grey stone, off-white, sage).
- **Bundle / variation strategy:** Launch **1-pack (S)** at ~€12.90 and a **2-pack (S + L, mixed neutrals)** at ~€22.90. Do not launch 3-pack until reviews are established — cannibalisation risk on first launch.
- **Estimated risk level:** **Low–Medium** (materials complaints are common; sample QC is essential)

Selling the top sheet **as a single SKU** (not the full set) is unusual on Amazon.es and solves a real customer pain: replacing a worn top sheet without having to buy a whole matching set. Simple size tree (3 sizes: cama 90 / 105 / 135), single colour launch, low return risk.
- **Demand signals:** Steady, non-seasonal, replenishment item; Spanish households buy top sheets more often than the EU average.
- **Competition signals:** Under-served — most competitors sell **full sets** (bajera + encimera + funda), which is exactly why single-top-sheet listings can rank on niche keywords. Price band €14–€25 for a good percale top sheet.

  - Percale vs. sateen vs. microfibra confusion — hilos (thread count) not stated or exaggerated.

Search volume for "waffle towels" and "waffle bath towels" has been consistently strong over the last 12 months, and the category is not yet dominated by a single private-label seller.
- **Demand signals:**

## Do Not Pursue

### A. Microfibre Kitchen Towel Multi-Packs (8–12 pack)

**Why rejected:** Race-to-the-bottom commodity category. Dominated by Amazon-aggregator brands (Amazon Basics, Utopia Towels, etc.) and dozens of Chinese sellers competing on price under £8. Margins are crushed, differentiation is essentially zero (microfibre is microfibre), and the category attracts price-sensitive shoppers who don't value brand. Doesn't fit Rosanube's premium-leaning positioning.

### B. Memory Foam Bath Mats

**Why rejected:** High return risk (mould complaints, edges curling, foam degrading), heavy to ship (kills margin on FBA), and the top spots are owned by established players (Genteele, Yimobra) with deep review moats. Buyer expectations vs. reality gap is wide, leading to 1- and 2-star reviews that are very hard to overcome as a new entrant.

### C. Baby Muslin Swaddle Blankets

**Why rejected:** Although textile-adjacent and lightweight, baby products carry meaningful certification overhead in the UK (CE / UKCA labelling, REACH compliance for dyes, infant-product safety standards). The category is also dominated by aden + anais and Mori. The combination of compliance burden, dominant incumbents, and high emotional risk if anything goes wrong (returns, complaints, recalls) makes this a poor fit for a daily-test private label.

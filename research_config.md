# For a product to continue validation, target:

#

# \* FBA fee: not too high relative to selling price

# \* Supplier cost: low enough to survive price competition

# \* Net margin is below 10% after realistic PPC/returns

# \* FBA fees destroy margin

# \* Supplier cost is too high

# \* Selling price is too low

# \* Competitors are racing to the bottom

# \* Product needs too many variations

# \* Returns are likely high

# \* Compliance risk is unclear

# Rosanube Amazon Product Research Config

## Brand

Rosanube

## Brand Focus

Rosanube sells home linen and home/kitchen textile products.

## Target Product Categories

* Bath towels
* Hand towels
* Kitchen towels
* Tea towels
* Bedsheets
* Pillow covers
* Duvet covers
* Table runners
* Bath mats
* Home/kitchen textile accessories

## Marketplace

Amazon UK

Change this later if needed:

* Amazon US
* Amazon UK
* Amazon India
* Amazon UAE
* Amazon Germany

## Avoid Products

Do not recommend:

* Electronics
* Fragile products
* Very heavy products
* Oversized products
* Products with high return risk
* Products dominated by big brands
* Products requiring complicated certifications
* Products with unclear legal or safety restrictions
* Products with very low differentiation opportunity

## Ideal Product Profile

Look for products that are:

* Lightweight or medium-weight
* Easy to ship
* Easy to bundle
* Suitable for private label
* Not too seasonal
* Useful for home, kitchen, bathroom, or bedroom
* Possible to improve through color, fabric, bundle size, design, packaging, or quality

## Research Priorities

Prioritize products with:

* Steady daily demand
* Manageable competition
* Weak existing listings
* Poor images from competitors
* Poor titles or bullet points from competitors
* Repeated customer complaints in reviews
* Clear opportunity to improve quality or packaging
* Good bundle potential
* Repeat purchase potential

## Product Scoring System

Score each product out of 50.

Use this structure:

* Demand: /10
* Competition: /10
* Differentiation opportunity: /10
* Estimated margin potential: /10
* Risk level: /10

Higher score means better opportunity.

## Risk Rules

Give a lower score if:

* Product has too many strong competitors
* Product has low selling price and weak margin
* Product is expensive to ship
* Product has high refund/return risk
* Product has many negative reviews about quality
* Product is dominated by one or two major brands
* Product requires certification or legal checks

## Daily Output Format

Every daily report should include:

1. Top 5 product opportunities
2. Why each product is interesting
3. Demand signals
4. Competition signals
5. Common weaknesses in existing listings
6. Differentiation ideas for Rosanube
7. Bundle or variation ideas
8. Risk level
9. Score out of 50
10. Best product to investigate today
11. Best low-risk test product
12. Products to avoid

## Important Rule

This research is only for shortlisting. Do not make final buying, sourcing, or inventory decisions without deeper manual verification.
